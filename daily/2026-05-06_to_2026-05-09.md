## 多模态代理型AI框架：用于环境重建与语义信道建模

原英文标题：A Multimodal Agentic AI Framework for Environmental Reconstruction and Semantic Channel Modeling

作者：Guangzheng Jing, Yixiao Tong, José Rodríguez-Piñeiro, Jingxiang Hong, Jiaxin Li, Siyang Tang, Xuefeng Yin

出版来源：IEEE Transactions on Cognitive Communications and Networking

出版时间：2026-05-04

原英文摘要：Integrated sensing and communication (ISAC) and AI-based channel models have emerged as key technologies in the future sixth generation (6G) systems. However, existing AI-based channel models suffer from limited generalization capability, as they often overlook the underlying propagation mechanisms, fail to characterize small-scale fading by ignoring multipath components (MPCs), and rely heavily on accurate three-dimensional (3D) environmental maps when based on raytracing approaches. This work proposes an Agentic AI based modeling methodology that integrates multimodal information, combining semantic attributes from the vision modality(the information obtained from photographs), spatial locations of clustersof- scattererss (CoSs) derived from channel data, and their geometric and topological relationships characterized by pivoted and hierarchical clusters within a unified framework. This design facilitates a comprehensive representation of multi-domain channel characteristics and offers a more accurate description of the underlying physical propagation mechanisms in complex environments, leading to improved generalization performance of the channel model. An intelligent clustering module groups MPCs by scatterer locations to extract CoS for target identification and environment reconstruction, while autonomously optimizing algorithms and parameters through a reward strategy according to the data characteristics. To validate the proposed method, a monostatic channel measurement campaign was conducted in a typical Urban Microcell (UMi) scenario. Experimental results demonstrate that the scatterers extracted from real channel data show strong correspondence with physical structures such as buildings and trees. The environment can thus be approximately reconstructed and semantically segmented. Finally, a semanticaware channel model is developed, which accounts for the physical propagation mechanisms influenced by the environmental information of the MPCs, without requiring an environmental map. This work establishes a standard agentic AI framework for future ISAC research, integrating data acquisition, parameter estimation, semantic channel analysis, and channel modeling.

中文摘要：集成感知与通信（ISAC）以及基于AI的信道模型已成为未来第六代（6G）系统的关键技术。然而，现有的基于AI的信道模型泛化能力有限，因为它们常常忽略底层传播机制、因忽略多径分量（MPCs）而无法刻画小尺度衰落，并且在基于射线追踪方法时需要依赖精确的三维（3D）环境地图。本文提出了一种基于代理型AI的建模方法，它集成多模态信息，将来自视觉模态（从照片中获取的信息）的语义属性、从信道数据中导出的散射簇（CoSs）的空间位置，以及通过枢轴和层次聚类表征的几何与拓扑关系统一在一个框架内。该设计有助于全面表征多域信道特性，并在复杂环境中更准确地描述底层物理传播机制，从而提升信道模型的泛化性能。一个智能聚类模块按散射体位置对MPCs进行分组，提取CoS用于目标识别和环境重建，并通过奖励策略根据数据特征自主优化算法和参数。为验证所提方法，在典型的城市微蜂窝（UMi）场景中进行了单站信道测量实验。实验结果表明，从真实信道数据中提取的散射体与建筑物、树木等物理结构有很强的对应关系。因此，环境可以被近似重建并进行语义分割。最后，本文开发了一种语义感知信道模型，它考虑了受MPCs环境信息影响的物理传播机制，而无需环境地图。该工作为未来的ISAC研究建立了一个标准的代理型AI框架，集成了数据采集、参数估计、语义信道分析和信道建模。

## 一种用于真实3D皮肤建模及其毫米波暴露分析的新型框架

原英文标题：A Novel Framework for Realistic 3D Skin Modeling and its Exposure to Millimeter Waves

作者：Serafeim Iakovidis, Athanasios Manassas, Myles Capstick, Christine Pich-Bavastro, Zhouxing Su, Dorothea Kapoukranidou, Olivier Gaide, Theodoros Samaras

出版来源：IEEE Journal of Microwaves

出版时间：2026-05-04

原英文摘要：Millimeter wave (MMW) technology is increasingly used in telecommunications and medical applications, necessitating a deeper understanding of its interaction with human tissue, particularly skin. The limited penetration depth of MMWs makes the skin the primary site of energy absorption, requiring detailed dosimetric analysis. Traditional skin models often assume planar, layered structures, neglecting the natural undulating geometry of the epidermis and dermis. This study introduces a novel methodological framework for constructing realistic three-dimensional (3D) human skin models using Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) and Ultrasound (US) imaging. The framework involves three key steps: (1) OCT and US image acquisition, (2) image segmentation and processing, and (3) synthesis of processed data to generate high-fidelity 3D skin models. Three anatomical sites—the volar forearm, hypothenar region, and fingertip—were selected for model development, representing variations in skin roughness and layer composition. The dielectric properties of the skin and subcutaneous layers were incorporated into finite-difference time-domain (FDTD) simulations at 27.5 GHz to evaluate the impact of skin roughness on electromagnetic field distribution. Our results demonstrate that the natural undulations of the dermoepidermal junction and air-skin interface significantly affect MMW energy absorption patterns. The roughness of these interfaces varies across anatomical sites, influencing local electric field distributions and leading to deviations from predictions based on planar models. The findings suggest that incorporating realistic skin geometries enhances dosimetric accuracy, which is critical for both safety assessments and optimizing MMW-based medical applications. This study provides an approach for individualized MMW dosimetry and underscores the importance of considering anatomical variations in electromagnetic exposure assessments. The framework was additionally extended to a nodular basal cell carcinoma case, revealing marginal changes in local field and SAR distributions and a moderate increase in reflected power compared to healthy skin.

中文摘要：毫米波（MMW）技术在电信和医疗领域的应用日益广泛，加深对其与人体组织（特别是皮肤）相互作用的理解变得至关重要。MMW穿透深度有限，使皮肤成为能量吸收的主要部位，因此需要详细的剂量学分析。传统皮肤模型通常假设为平面分层结构，忽略了表皮和真皮的自然起伏几何形态。本研究提出了一种新颖的方法学框架，利用光学相干断层扫描（OCT）和超声（US）成像构建真实的三维（3D）人体皮肤模型。该框架包含三个关键步骤：(1) OCT和US图像采集，(2) 图像分割与处理，(3) 合成处理后的数据生成高保真3D皮肤模型。选取前臂掌侧、小鱼际区和指尖三个解剖部位进行模型开发，这些部位代表不同的皮肤粗糙度和层组成。将皮肤及皮下层的介电特性纳入27.5 GHz的时域有限差分（FDTD）仿真中，以评估皮肤粗糙度对电磁场分布的影响。结果表明，真皮-表皮交界处和空气-皮肤界面的自然起伏显著影响MMW能量吸收模式。这些界面的粗糙度随解剖部位而异，影响局部电场分布，并导致与平面模型预测结果的偏差。研究提示，纳入真实的皮肤几何形状可提高剂量学准确性，这对安全评估和优化基于MMW的医疗应用都至关重要。本研究为个性化MMW剂量学提供了方法，并强调了在电磁暴露评估中考虑解剖变异的重要性。该框架还扩展至一个结节性基底细胞癌病例，显示与健康皮肤相比，局部场和SAR分布出现微小变化，反射功率适度增加。

## 自适应3D-RoPE：面向无线基础模型的物理对齐旋转位置编码

原英文标题：Adaptive 3D-RoPE: Physics-Aligned Rotary Positional Encoding for Wireless Foundation Models

作者：Chenyu Zhang, Xinchen Lyu, Chenshan Ren, Shuhan Liu, Qimei Cui

出版来源：arXiv

出版时间：2026-05-01

原英文摘要：Positional encoding plays a pivotal role in determin?ing the extrapolation and generalization performance of wireless foundation models for channel state information (CSI) modeling, latent characterization, and task-specific prediction. However, existing CSI models inherit static or one-dimensional positional priors from natural language and vision architectures, which fundamentally misalign with the intrinsic physics of wireless channels by lacking explicit relative decay, collapsing the 3D spatio-temporal-frequency structure, and remaining scenario?rigid. This paper proposes Adaptive 3D-RoPE, a physics-aligned rotary positional encoding that establishes the structural corner?stone for wireless foundation models. The framework integrates a learnable, axis-decoupled 3D frequency bank to explicitly disentangle multi-dimensional phase dependencies, coupled with a lightweight channel-conditioned controller that dynamically modulates the prior via compact global CSI descriptors. This sample-adaptive mechanism transforms positional encoding from a static transformer component into a dynamic, coherence-aware inductive bias to resolve heterogeneous channel physics. Extensive experiments across 100 datasets demonstrate the superiority of the proposed scheme in both scale extrapolation and zero-shot generalization. Compared to the state-of-the-art, our method achieves up to a 10.7 dB reduction in normalized mean square error (NMSE) under 8 times antenna scale extrapolation. Given the same CSI input scales, our method can also improve zero-shot NMSE by 1.07 dB across unseen mobility scenarios and 0.90 dB in low-frequency-to-millimeter-wave tasks.

中文摘要：位置编码在决定用于信道状态信息（CSI）建模、潜在特征表征和特定任务预测的无线基础模型的外推和泛化性能中起着关键作用。然而，现有的CSI模型从自然语言和视觉架构中继承了静态或一维的位置先验，这些先验在根本上与无线信道的固有物理特性不匹配：缺乏明确的相对衰减、压缩了3D时空-频率结构，并且保持场景刚性。本文提出自适应3D-RoPE，一种物理对齐的旋转位置编码，为无线基础模型建立了结构基石。该框架集成了一个可学习的、轴解耦的3D频率库，以显式解耦多维相位依赖关系，并结合了一个轻量级的信道条件控制器，通过紧凑的全局CSI描述符动态调制先验。这种样本自适应机制将位置编码从静态transformer组件转变为动态的、相干感知的归纳偏置，以解决异构信道物理特性。跨越100个数据集的大量实验证明了所提方案在尺度外推和零样本泛化方面的优越性。与最先进方法相比，我们的方法在天线尺度外推8倍的情况下，归一化均方误差（NMSE）降低了高达10.7 dB。在相同的CSI输入尺度下，我们的方法还可以在未见过的移动性场景中将零样本NMSE提升1.07 dB，在低频至毫米波任务中提升0.90 dB。

## AO-RMM：一种用于毫米波系统中联合IRS相位和预编码设计的可扩展黎曼优化框架

原英文标题：AO-RMM: A Scalable Riemannian Optimization Framework for Joint IRS Phase and Precoding Design in mmWave Systems

作者：Mian Muhammad Kamal, Syed Zain Ul Abideen, Ijaz Khan, Basem Abu Zneid, Aadel Mohammed Alatwi

出版来源：IEEE Open Journal of the Communications Society

出版时间：2026-05-04

原英文摘要：Intelligent reflecting surfaces (IRS) represent a promising paradigm for augmenting millimetre-wave (mmWave) multi-user communication systems through highly advanced control of the radio propagation environment. Nevertheless, simultaneously optimizing IRS phase shifts and transmitting precoding matrices remains computationally intensive due to the non-convexity of the objective and the unit-modulus constraints imposed on the IRS elements. To overcome these limitations without increasing the linear computational complexity, this paper proposes an alternating optimization Riemannian majorization-minimization (AO-RMM) methodology. The AO-RMM algorithm exploits the geometric properties of the complex unit circle to solve IRS phase constraints, and, using a majorization-minimization scheme, restates the non-convex problem in question as a sequence of convex subproblems. In comparison to the traditional semidefinite programming (SDP) approaches, which experience poor scalability and large computation cost, the proposed solution is linear in terms of the number of units to deploy per population, that is, it can be applied in large-scale deployments. Extensive simulation studies demonstrate that the AO-RMM framework provides a significant increase in aggregate throughput, even compared to randomly configured IRS setups, across a wide range of system parameters. The algorithm exhibits strong convergence behaviour, typically requiring 50 to 100 iterations, and remains effective even under discrete phase quantization. Scalability tests confirm that the throughput gains persist and even increase for IRS panels with up to 256 elements, validating the framework’s applicability to next-generation wireless architectures.

中文摘要：智能反射面（IRS）通过高度先进的无线电传播环境控制，为增强毫米波（mmWave）多用户通信系统提供了一种有前景的范式。然而，由于目标函数的非凸性以及对IRS元素施加的单位模约束，同时优化IRS相移和发射预编码矩阵在计算上仍然非常密集。为了克服这些限制而不增加线性计算复杂度，本文提出了一种交替优化黎曼主要化-最小化（AO-RMM）方法。AO-RMM算法利用复单位圆的几何性质来解决IRS相位约束，并通过主要化-最小化方案将所考虑的非凸问题重新表述为一系列凸子问题。与传统的半定规划（SDP）方法相比——后者可扩展性差且计算成本高——所提出的解决方案在部署单元数量上具有线性复杂度，即可应用于大规模部署。广泛的仿真研究表明，即使与随机配置的IRS设置相比，AO-RMM框架在广泛的系统参数下也能显著提高总吞吐量。该算法表现出强收敛性，通常需要50到100次迭代，并且即使在离散相位量化下也保持有效。可扩展性测试证实，对于多达256个元素的IRS面板，吞吐量增益持续存在甚至增加，验证了该框架在下一代无线架构中的适用性。

原文链接：https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/abstract/document/11505819

## MAEPose：基于毫米波视频的人体姿态估计的自监督时空学习

原英文标题：MAEPose: Self-Supervised Spatiotemporal Learning for Human Pose Estimation on mmWave Video

作者：Xijia Wei, Yuan Fang, Kevin Chetty, Youngjun Cho, Nadia Bianchi-Berthouze

出版来源：arXiv

出版时间：2026-04-30

原英文摘要：Millimetre-wave (mmWave) radar offers a more privacy-preserving alternative to RGB-based human pose estimation. However, existing methods typically rely on pre-extracted intermediate representations such as sparse point clouds or spectrogram images, where the rich spatiotemporal information naturally present in radar video streams is discarded for model learning, while such signal processing adds system complexity. In addition, existing solutions are mainly conducted in an end-to-end supervised manner without leveraging unlabelled raw video streams to learn generalized representations. In this study, we present MAEPose, a masked autoencoding-based human pose estimation approach that operates directly on mmWave spectrogram videos. MAEPose learns spatiotemporal motion-aware generalized representations from unlabelled radar video, and leverages its heatmap decoder for multi-frame pose estimation predictions. We evaluate it across three datasets based on leave-one-person-out cross-validation with rigorous statistical testing.
