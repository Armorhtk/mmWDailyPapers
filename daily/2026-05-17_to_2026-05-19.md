## 带自适应稀疏处理的4D雷达加速器用于实时目标检测

原英文标题：A 4D Radar Accelerator with Adaptive Sparse Processing for Real-Time Object Detection

作者：Sung Eun Kim, Chang Eun Song, Byeonggon Kang, Maria Sojan, Omkar Sathe, Tajana Rosing, Mingu Kang

出版来源：2026 IEEE Custom Integrated Circuits Conference (CICC)

出版时间：2026-04-23

原英文摘要：This work presents an ultra-low power hardware accelerator tailored for real-time 4D radar-based 3D object detection. We introduce a radar-optimized pruning framework that removes redundant activations and weights using structured weight pruning and importance-based run-time input pruning. A sparse-efficient Processing Element (PE) combined with the proposed Dynamic Accumulation Unit (DAU) enables neighbor-search-free processing of dual-sparse data in both activations and weights with minimal hardware overhead while maintaining accuracy. These techniques are applied across all backbone layers and integrated into a 65 nm silicon prototype. The chip achieves 2.55TOPS/W, providing 1.48× higher energy efficiency and 2.71× higher area efficiency compared to state-of-the-art 3D point cloud accelerators, validating its effectiveness for next-generation reliable and low-power 3D object detection.

## 面向4-D毫米波汽车雷达应用的新型MIMO塑料金属化波导缝隙天线阵

原英文标题：A Novel MIMO Plastic Metallized Waveguide Slot Antenna Array for 4-D Millimeter-Wave Automotive Radar Applications

作者：Qian Chen, Xu Chu, Xinyue Guo, Minjia Chai, Wei Peng, Yulu Zhang, Yun He

出版来源：IEEE Access

出版时间：2026-05-12

原英文摘要：This paper presents a novel multiple input multiple output (MIMO) plastic metallized waveguide slot antenna array for 4-D millimeter-wave (MMW) automotive radar. This MIMO antenna array comprises 12 stepped-arranged transmitters and 16 linear-arranged receivers, forming a parallelogram virtual array with 192 channels and enabling high-angular resolution for automotive radar. The transmitting (TX) and receiving (RX) antennas are plastic metallized ridge waveguide slot antennas with 8 slots. The plastic metallized TX and RX antennas weigh 3.6 g and 5 g, respectively, which are 24.7 g and 27.1 g lighter than their aluminum samples. Measurement results show that the antenna units achieve a wide operational bandwidth of 74-82 GHz. The measured gains of the TX and RX antennas at 75-81 GHz are 10.2-12.4 dBi and 11.8-14.9 dBi, respectively. Excluding the transmission loss by the feeding lines of the test board, the radiation efficiency of the TX and RX antennas at 75-81 GHz is expected to be 59.8%-93.8% and 79.6%-92.7%, respectively. The measured isolation of the adjacent TX and RX antennas exceeds 40 dB and 30 dB, and the measured isolation between the TX and RX antennas exceeds 50 dB at 75-81 GHz. A novel 4-D MMW automotive radar based on the proposed MIMO array is designed, fabricated, and measured. This radar achieves angular resolutions of 2.1° in azimuth and 4.7° in elevation. The proposed 4-D MMW radar offers a viable solution for new-generation high-resolution, high isolation, high radiation efficiency, high gain, high integration, and lightweight automotive radar.

出版来源：2026 17th German Microwave Conference (GeMiC)

出版时间：2026-03-11

原英文摘要：This paper presents a compact antenna measurement setup designed for D-band applications. The setup integrates a robotic arm, a millimeter-wave radar module, and a parabolic reflector, enabling flexible characterization of antennas. The shift towards higher operating frequency offers increased usable bandwidth. While this leads to higher range resolution and higher data rates in sensing and communication applications, the aditional available bandwith can also be leveraged to enable much more compact antenna test solutions. Contrary, millimeter wave systems often utilize high gain antennas to overcome increased free-space attenuation, causing large far-field distances that limit dimensions of the antenna measurement setup. Therefore, the proposed compact test range uses the near-field properties of a parabolic reflector to create a planar wavefront in a confined space. This setup allows for antenna measurements under quasi-far-field conditions. A key element of the setup is an electromagnetic switch placed near the focal region. This switch allows the relevant target peak to be isolated within the radar measurements, thereby improving measurement clarity and repeatability. Simulations and measurements validate the concepts suitability.

## 一种创新的半参数密度模型用于地面车辆雷达散射截面的统计表征

原英文标题：An Innovative Semiparametric Density Model for the Statistical Characterization of Ground-Vehicle Radar Cross Sections

作者：Zengcan Liu, Shuhao Wen, Houjun Sun, Ming Deng

出版来源：Sensors

出版时间：2026-04-22

原英文摘要：Accurately characterizing the statistical fluctuations of vehicle radar cross sections (RCSs) across polarization states and azimuthal sectors is essential for evaluating detection performance, conducting probabilistic simulations, and analyzing target features in millimeter-wave radar systems. Existing one-dimensional RCS statistical models, including Weibull, Chi-square, Lognormal, Rice, and Gaussian distributions, are often limited by their restricted functional expressiveness, making it difficult to simultaneously capture skewness, tail thickness, and azimuthal dependence under narrow angular-domain conditions. In addition, purely nonparametric approaches tend to produce spurious modes under finite-sample conditions and lack interpretable structural priors. To address these limitations, this paper proposes a Unimodal RCS Semiparametric Density Estimator (URCS-SDE) tailored for ground-vehicle targets. The proposed approach adopts kernel density estimation (KDE) as a data-driven baseline representation and incorporates physically plausible structural constraints through unimodal shape projection. Then a beta-type tail template is further introduced in the normalized amplitude domain to regulate boundary decay behavior. Finally, weighted least-squares calibration is performed on the histogram grid of the empirical probability density function (PDF), achieving a balanced trade-off between fitting accuracy and stability in both the peak and tail regions. Using multi-azimuth RCS measurements of two representative ground vehicles, the URCS-SDE is systematically compared with five classical parametric distributions and a representative regularized mixture density network (MDN) baseline. Performance is evaluated under both full-azimuth and directional-window conditions using the sum of squared errors (SSE), root mean squared error (RMSE), coefficient of determination (R-square) and held-out negative log-likelihood (NLL). The results show that the URCS-SDE consistently provides the most accurate and stable density estimates, especially in narrow angular windows. In addition, a threshold-based detection-support example derived from the fitted PDFs demonstrates that the advantage of the URCS-SDE transfers from density reconstruction to a directly engineering-relevant downstream quantity.

中文摘要：准确表征车辆雷达散射截面（RCS）在不同极化状态和方位扇区内的统计波动，对于评估毫米波雷达系统的检测性能、执行概率仿真以及分析目标特征至关重要。现有的单维RCS统计模型，包括Weibull、Chi-square、Lognormal、Rice和Gaussian分布，往往受限于其有限的功能表达能力，难以在窄角度域条件下同时捕捉偏度、尾厚和方位依赖性。此外，纯非参数方法在有限样本条件下容易产生虚假模态，且缺乏可解释的结构先验。为解决这些局限，本文提出一种针对地面车辆目标的单峰RCS半参数密度估计器（URCS-SDE）。该方法采用核密度估计（KDE）作为数据驱动的基线表示，并通过单峰形状投影引入物理上合理的结构约束。然后在归一化幅度域中进一步引入beta型尾部模板以调节边界衰减行为。最后，在经验概率密度函数（PDF）的直方图网格上执行加权最小二乘校准，实现了峰值和尾部区域拟合精度与稳定性之间的平衡折中。利用两辆代表性地面车辆的多方位RCS测量值，将URCS-SDE与五种经典参数分布及一种代表性的正则化混合密度网络（MDN）基线进行了系统比较。性能评估在全方位和定向窗口条件下进行，使用平方误差和（SSE）、均方根误差（RMSE）、决定系数（R-square）和留出负对数似然（NLL）。结果表明，URCS-SDE始终提供最准确且稳定的密度估计，尤其在窄角度窗口中。此外，基于拟合PDF的阈值检测支持示例表明，URCS-SDE的优势从密度重建转移到了直接与工程相关的下游量。

原英文摘要：Autonomous driving demands robust 3D perception under challenging conditions. ## 云边协同的60 GHz FMCW雷达用于工业液位感知

原英文摘要：To address the issues of significant data drift and high maintenance costs of traditional liquid level sensors in harsh environments such as high pressure, corrosion, and high dust in modern process industries, this paper proposes a non-contact level perception solution based on 60 GHz Frequency Modulated Continuous Wave (FMCW) and a cloud-edge collaborative architecture.
