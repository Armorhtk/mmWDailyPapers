## 3D融合测量仪：利用雷达-相机融合传感器实现精确全场三维定位与位移测量

原英文标题：3D fusion meter: enabling accurate full-field 3D localization and displacement measurement with a fused radar-camera sensor

作者：Yingjie Gou, Yuyong Xiong, Sicheng Hong, Yongliang Zhang, Wendi Tian, Xi Liang, Zhike Peng

出版来源：Mechanical Systems and Signal Processing

出版时间：2026-05-05

原英文摘要：Achieving accurate, full-field, and contactless three-dimensional (3D) localization and displacement measurement remains a persistent challenge in vibration monitoring and engineering systems. Conventional vision-based systems suffer from limited depth accuracy, whereas radar-based approaches, although highly accurate along the line-of-sight, exhibit poor lateral measurement accuracy and typically require complex sensor networks to enable full-field 3D sensing. To this end, we propose a unified heterogeneous sensing framework that fuses a millimeter-wave radar with a monocular camera, thereby creating a unique 3D Fusion Meter, which is capable of accurate full-field 3D localization and displacement measurement. To achieve accurate alignment between the heterogeneous sensors, a robust calibration framework based on joint pose optimization is first proposed. Subsequently, a composite target with pronounced microwave reflectivity and distinctive visual features is designed to support consistent cross-modal target perception and measurement. Building upon this target, a 3D localization method and a corresponding 3D displacement reconstruction algorithm are established to exploit the complementary measurement characteristics of microwave and vision sensing. Experimental validations conducted at both near and far ranges confirm that the proposed fusion-based measurement approach achieves accurate full-field 3D localization and high-fidelity 3D displacement reconstruction, providing a low-complexity, in-situ, and real-time solution for vibration analysis and structural health monitoring.

中文摘要：在振动监测与工程系统中，实现精确、全场、非接触的三维定位与位移测量仍是一个持续挑战。传统基于视觉的系统受限于深度精度不足，而基于雷达的方法虽在视线方向上精度很高，但横向测量精度较差，且通常需要复杂的传感器网络才能实现全场三维感知。为此，我们提出一种统一的异质传感框架，融合毫米波雷达与单目相机，从而创建独特的3D融合测量仪，能够实现精确的全场三维定位与位移测量。为实现异质传感器间的精确对齐，首先提出一种基于联合位姿优化的稳健标定框架。随后，设计了一种具有显著微波反射率与独特视觉特征的复合目标，以支持一致的跨模态目标感知与测量。基于此目标，建立了三维定位方法及相应的三维位移重构算法，以充分利用微波与视觉传感的互补测量特性。在近距和远距范围内进行的实验验证表明，所提出的基于融合的测量方法实现了精确的全场三维定位与高保真三维位移重构，为振动分析与结构健康监测提供了一种低复杂度、原位实时的解决方案。

## 伯努利粒子滤波器：雷达探测遮挡环境中的目标跟踪

原英文标题：A Bernoulli Particle Filter for Target Tracking in a Radar Detection-Occluded Environment

作者：Shuai Sun, Guchong Li, Hongguang Lyu

出版来源：IEEE Journal of Oceanic Engineering

出版时间：2026-05-05

原英文摘要：In maritime environments, target detection using radar signals often suffers from occlusion by islands or other vessels, particularly in where the radar platform is a small autonomous surface vehicle (ASV) with a civil marine radar with a low-height viewing angle. Low antenna height on a ASV platform can significantly reduce the spatial coverage of the radar beam as a result of signal occlusion. Once a high value target is occluded, continuous tracking of it may be interrupted because of the lack of radar measurements. In the literature, target occlusion is often ignored or only partly addressed for a specific target tracking scenario. This article proposes to use Bernoulli random finite sets to model the appearance and disappearance of a target in a radar detection occluded environment, and a particle-based implementation to produce an algorithm with environmental occlusion awareness. More specifically, we first segment the environment using uniform grid cells and then check the line-of-sight condition between each particle and the radar platform for occlusion. This method is tested using both simulated and real radar data. The results demonstrate that our proposed solution outperforms existing trackers that ignore target occlusion.

中文摘要：在海洋环境中，使用雷达信号进行目标探测常因岛屿或其他船只的遮挡而受到影响，尤其是在雷达平台为小型自主水面艇（ASV）且配备低视角民用航海雷达的情况下。ASV平台上天线高度较低会因信号遮挡显著降低雷达波束的空间覆盖范围。一旦高价值目标被遮挡，由于缺乏雷达测量数据，对其连续跟踪可能中断。现有文献中，目标遮挡常常被忽略或仅针对特定目标跟踪场景部分解决。本文提出使用伯努利随机有限集来建模雷达探测遮挡环境中目标的出现与消失，并基于粒子实现构建一种具有环境遮挡感知能力的算法。具体而言，我们首先使用均匀网格对环境进行分割，然后检查每个粒子与雷达平台之间的视线条件以判断遮挡。该方法已在仿真数据和真实雷达数据上进行了测试。结果表明，我们提出的方案优于忽略目标遮挡的现有跟踪器。

## 一种用于毫米波应用的紧凑型超宽带MIMO天线

作者：Zijian Dong, Zhenzhen Jiang, Mark Leach, Jiafeng Zhou

出版来源：2026 IEEE International Workshop on Antenna Technology (iWAT)

出版时间：2026-03-27

原英文摘要：This paper presents an ultrawideband (UWB) millimetre-wave (mmWave) multiple-input, multiple-output (MIMO) antenna employing a defected ground structure (DGS) covering the 5G NR bands n257, n258, n259, n260, and n262. The proposed design has a compact volume of $28 \times 26 \times 0.832 ~\text{mm}^{3}$ and achieves an operating bandwidth from 23.71 to 51.99 GHz, corresponding to a 74.71% fractional bandwidth. The MIMO configuration exhibits strong diversity performance; an envelope correlation coefficient below 0.06 and diversity gain above 9.97 dB. The antenna achieves radiation and total efficiencies exceeding 90% and 80%, respectively, and provides a maximum gain of 6.45 dBi. The measured and simulated $S$ -parameter results are in good agreement, showing the proposed design can be used for 5G mm wave applications.

中文摘要：本文提出了一种采用缺陷地结构（DGS）的超宽带（UWB）毫米波（mmWave）多输入多输出（MIMO）天线，覆盖5G NR频段n257、n258、n259、n260和n262。该设计具有紧凑的体积 $28 \times 26 \times 0.832 ~\text{mm}^{3}$，实现了从23.71到51.99 GHz的工作带宽，对应74.71%的分数带宽。MIMO配置表现出强分集性能：包络相关系数低于0.06，分集增益高于9.97 dB。天线的辐射效率和总效率分别超过90%和80%，最大增益为6.45 dBi。测量和仿真的S参数结果吻合良好，表明该设计可用于5G毫米波应用。

## 基于MIMO雷达与深度学习的手势空中书写系统

作者：Xiangrong Wang, Hengfeng Liu, Moeness G. Amin, Kaiquan Cai, Yanbo Zhu

出版来源：Radar-Based Human-Computer Interaction

出版时间：2026-05-03

原英文摘要：Recently, radar-based Hand Gesture Recognition (HGR) has gained increased attention in several applications involving contactless Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). Air-writing, as a definite HGR, requires real-time target positioning and trajectory tracking, followed by alphanumerical recognition via deep learning. Benefiting from the large virtual array provided by Multiple Input Multiple Output (MIMO) radar, this chapter presents an interferometry-based processing approach to acquire the subtle range and azimuth displacements of finger motions, thus fulfilling the tracking of the alphanumerical trajectory. A ResNet50 Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) trained with the trajectory is used to recognise the correct writings. Additionally, spatial interferometry is exploited to identify multiple strokes when writing specific characters, punctuations or words requiring non-contiguous hand motion articulation. This is achieved by utilizing the subtle elevation change induced by hand lifts among different strokes. Experimental results show that the proposed air-writing system performs rather well in sensing and tracking hand movements, achieving high recognition accuracy for different types of air writings.

## 基于模型驱动的深度学习CPM信道估计方法

原英文标题：A Model-Driven Deep Learning-Based Channel Estimation Method for CPM

作者：Le Wang, Junhao Li

出版来源：IEEE Communications Letters

出版时间：2026-05-05

原英文摘要：Continuous phase modulation (CPM) offers significant advantages in power-efficient transmission systems due to its constant envelope. Under wideband transmission, an accurate channel estimation is critical for subsequent processing. Traditional channel estimation methods suffer from either high complexity or poor performance under limited pilots. Compressed sensing (CS) techniques have been widely adopted in channel estimation to exploit the inherent sparsity of multipath channels, thereby enabling accurate channel recovery with a reduced pilot overhead. Among them, the approximate message passing (AMP) algorithm is particularly attractive due to its low complexity. However, it is prone to divergence when measurement matrices are non-i.i.d. Gaussian. While vector AMP (VAMP) improves stability, it incurs high computational costs due to matrix inversions and remains sensitive to prior mismatches. Data-driven learning-based methods are attractive for their robustness but are unexplainable and unpredictable. To address these issues, we propose CPM-NN-AMP, a complex model-driven deep learning (MDDL) framework. We unfold the VAMP algorithm into a deep network and replace the linear estimator with a learnable non-linear module. Additionally, we introduce a unified complex radial gain formulation (UCRGF) to design complex-valued shrinkage functions that effectively preserve phase information. Simulation results demonstrate that the proposed method achieves nearly 7 dB of gain over conventional CS-based methods and outperforms other learning-based approaches across various complex multipath channels.

中文摘要：连续相位调制（CPM）因其恒包络特性在功率高效传输系统中具有显著优势。在宽带传输中，准确的信道估计对后续处理至关重要。传统信道估计方法在导频受限时要么复杂度高，要么性能差。压缩感知（CS）技术被广泛用于信道估计，利用多径信道固有的稀疏性，从而在减少导频开销的情况下实现准确的信道恢复。其中，近似消息传递（AMP）算法因其低复杂度而特别有吸引力。然而，当测量矩阵非独立同分布高斯时，它容易发散。向量AMP（VAMP）提高了稳定性，但由于矩阵求逆导致计算成本高，并且仍对先验失配敏感。数据驱动的基于学习的方法因其鲁棒性而具有吸引力，但缺乏可解释性和可预测性。为解决这些问题，我们提出了CPM-NN-AMP，一种复数模型驱动的深度学习（MDDL）框架。我们将VAMP算法展开成深度网络，并用可学习的非线性模块替代线性估计器。此外，我们引入了统一的复数径向增益公式（UCRGF）来设计复值收缩函数，有效保留相位信息。仿真结果表明，所提方法相比传统基于CS的方法实现了近7 dB的增益，并在各种复杂多径信道下优于其他基于学习的方法。

## 一种使用毫米波雷达的工业目标跟踪新滤波方法

原英文标题：A Novel Filtering Method for Industrial Target Tracking Using Millimeter-Wave Radar

作者：Chenwei Fan, Rui Zheng, Shuhong Liang, Min Wu, Xilin Rui, Zibao Lu, Fangdong Li, Xiaoyao Zheng

出版来源：IEEE Sensors Journal

出版时间：2026-05-05

原英文摘要：In this paper, a novel target tracking and filtering method is proposed to address the challenges of millimeter-wave radar perception in industrial environments, including multipath interference, high noise levels, and stringent real-time requirements. A velocity-constrained multidimensional Density-Based Spatial Clustering of Applications with Noise (DBSCAN) clustering algorithm is developed to improve clustering accuracy and stability by exploiting the triangle inequality to accelerate processing and reduce false alarms. For target tracking, a constant-velocity (CV) motion model is adopted, and radial velocity information is incorporated to derive a nonlinear measurement equation for constructing an extended Kalman filter (EKF) framework. Experimental results demonstrate that the proposed method improves clustering accuracy by 19%, achieves 94% clustering stability, and increases clustering speed by approximately eight times. Additionally, compared with the DBSCAN+KF-based tracking method and the GTRACK method, the proposed method achieves a higher tracking success rate and more accurate robot tracking.

中文摘要：本文提出了一种新颖的目标跟踪与滤波方法，以应对工业环境中毫米波雷达感知面临的挑战，包括多径干扰、高噪声水平以及严格的实时性要求。通过利用三角不等式加速处理并减少虚警，开发了一种速度约束的多维基于密度的噪声应用空间聚类（DBSCAN）算法，以提高聚类的准确性和稳定性。在目标跟踪方面，采用了恒定速度（CV）运动模型，并引入径向速度信息推导出非线性测量方程，用于构建扩展卡尔曼滤波器（EKF）框架。实验结果表明，所提方法将聚类准确率提高了19%，聚类稳定性达到94%，聚类速度提升了约8倍。此外，与基于DBSCAN+KF的跟踪方法和GTRACK方法相比，所提方法实现了更高的跟踪成功率和更精确的机器人跟踪。
