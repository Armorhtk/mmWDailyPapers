## 0cal：毫米波网络零成本校准方法

原英文标题：0cal: Zero-Cost Calibration for mmWave Networks

作者：Xin Liu, Wei-Han Chen, Kannan Srinivasan

出版来源：Proceedings of the 2026 ACM/IEEE International Conference on Embedded Artificial Intelligence and Sensing Systems

出版时间：2026-05-10

原英文摘要：Antenna array calibration is essential for mmWave networks to deliver multi-gigabit throughput, especially for emerging AI-driven applications like immersive XR. However, conventional calibration pipelines rely on laboratory-grade over-the-air (OTA) test cells and specialized instruments, which must be deployed per device and used repeatedly over its lifetime. These requirements significantly increase the cost and complexity of large-scale mmWave deployments. In this paper, we present 0cal, a novel technique that operates purely using everyday communication, without laboratory environments, external sensors, or hardware modifications. The core idea of 0cal is to decouple constant relative antenna gains from the variable Angle of Departure (AoD) encoded in routine signals. By leveraging the statistical diversity of in-field traffic, 0cal averages out AoD-dependent sinusoidal terms while preserving gain-dependent components. It further incorporates a codebook-based selection algorithm to synthesize an approximately uniform effective AoD subset from accumulated communication instances, ensuring robust performance even with skewed AoD distributions. We implement 0cal on an open-source mmWave platform; experimental results show that after approximately 700 instances, 0cal achieves SNR performance comparable to laboratory-based methods, demonstrating its practicality for large-scale deployments.

中文摘要：天线阵列校准对于毫米波网络实现多千兆吞吐量至关重要，尤其是针对新兴的AI驱动应用（如沉浸式XR）。然而，传统校准流程依赖实验室级空中测试单元和专用仪器，必须为每台设备部署并在其生命周期内重复使用。这些要求显著增加了大规模毫米波部署的成本和复杂性。本文提出0cal，一种仅使用日常通信即可运行的新技术，无需实验室环境、外部传感器或硬件修改。0cal的核心思想是将恒定的相对天线增益与常规信号中编码的可变角度偏离解耦。通过利用现场业务流的统计多样性，0cal在保留增益相关成分的同时，平均化了依赖AoD的正弦项。它进一步引入基于代码本的选择算法，从累积的通信实例中合成近似均匀的有效AoD子集，确保即使在AoD分布偏斜的情况下也能实现稳健性能。我们在开源毫米波平台上实现了0cal；实验结果表明，经过约700个实例后，0cal的SNR性能与基于实验室的方法相当，证明了其在大规模部署中的实用性。

## QDA-SSM：基于雷达的搏动间隔/心率变异性检测的质量门控与延迟对齐状态空间框架

原英文标题：A Quality-Gating and Delay-Alignment State-Space Framework for Radar-Based IBI/HRV Detection

作者：Mu Li, Jianhua Zhou

出版来源：IEEE Access

出版时间：2026-05-08

原英文摘要：Inter-beat interval and heart rate variability are essential indicators of cardiovascular and autonomic function, yet their reliable estimation from radar signals remains challenging owing to time-varying observability, multipath interference, coherence collapse, and body motion. To address these issues, we propose a quality-gating and delay-aligned state-space framework (QDA-SSM) for non-contact beat-event detection, IBI estimation, and HRV estimation. The framework combines observability-aware quality gating to suppress unreliable phase segments, mask-gated state-space modeling with local enhancement to stabilize beat-event timestamps, and delay alignment for event-level supervision to reduce temporal mismatch between radar and ECG. Trained with a joint event–IBI–HRV objective, QDA-SSM is designed to jointly optimize local beat-event detection and interval-level statistical consistency. Evaluation on synchronized radar–ECG recordings from 14 subjects acquired primarily under controlled resting supine conditions showed that QDA-SSM achieved an IBI-MAE of 11.1 ms and an RMSSD-MAE of 8.4 ms under leave-one-subject-out evaluation, and 10.2 ms and 8.2 ms, respectively, under a chronological split protocol. Motion segments remained challenging, with coverage decreasing from approximately 95% at rest to 68% during movement, indicating that body motion remains a major failure mode under the present setup. Overall, these results support QDA-SSM as an interpretable pilot-scale framework for radar-based IBI and HRV estimation under controlled resting conditions in a subject-limited cohort, rather than as evidence of broad real-world generalization.

中文摘要：搏动间隔和心率变异性是心血管和自主神经功能的重要指标，但由于时变可观测性、多径干扰、相干性崩塌和身体运动，从雷达信号中可靠估计它们仍然具有挑战性。为了解决这些问题，我们提出了一种质量门控与延迟对齐状态空间框架（QDA-SSM），用于非接触式心跳事件检测、IBI估计和HRV估计。该框架结合了可观测性感知的质量门控以抑制不可靠的相位片段、带局部增强的掩码门控状态空间建模以稳定心跳事件时间戳，以及用于事件级监督的延迟对齐以减少雷达和心电图之间的时间错位。通过联合事件-IBI-HRV目标训练，QDA-SSM旨在联合优化局部心跳事件检测和间隔级统计一致性。对14名受试者在主要受控静息仰卧条件下采集的同步雷达-心电图记录进行评估，结果显示，在留一受试者交叉验证下，QDA-SSM实现了11.1 ms的IBI平均绝对误差和8.4 ms的RMSSD平均绝对误差；在按时间顺序划分方案下，则分别为10.2 ms和8.2 ms。运动时段仍然具有挑战性，覆盖率从静息时的约95%下降到运动时的68%，这表明在当前设置下身体运动仍然是主要的失效模式。总体而言，这些结果支持QDA-SSM作为在受控静息条件下、受试者有限队列中基于雷达的IBI和HRV估计的可解释试点级框架，而非广泛现实泛化的证据。

## 两阶段运动感知框架：基于毫米波雷达的人体网格重建

原英文标题：A Two-Stage Motion-Aware Framework for mmWave-based Human Mesh Recovery

作者：Hoang Hai Pham, Shuntian Zheng, Jiaqi Li, Yu Guan

出版来源：arXiv

出版时间：2026-05-08

原英文摘要：Millimeter-wave (mmWave) radar has emerged as a promising sensing modality for human perception due to its robustness under challenging environmental conditions and strong privacy-preserving properties. However, recovering accurate 3D human body meshes from radar observations remains difficult due to severe signal clutter and the inherently partial nature of radar measurements. Previous works typically adopt end-to-end frameworks that directly regress human body parameters from raw radar data, without decoupling signal interpretation from geometric reasoning or exploiting temporal motion cues, limiting learning performance. To address this, we propose a two-stage framework for radar-based human body reconstruction. First, we introduce a human reflection extraction module that performs coarse-to-fine localization and voxel-wise segmentation to produce a confidence-weighted radar volume encoding voxel-level human likelihood. Second, we design a motion-aware mesh recovery network that reconstructs the human body by jointly modeling per-frame geometry and inter-frame dynamics using a dual-branch architecture. Extensive experiments demonstrate that the proposed method outperforms existing approaches while maintaining computational efficiency.

中文摘要：毫米波（mmWave）雷达因其在恶劣环境条件下的鲁棒性和强隐私保护特性，已成为一种有前景的人体感知传感模态。然而，由于严重的信号杂波和雷达测量固有的局部性，从雷达观测中恢复准确的3D人体网格仍然困难。以往的工作通常采用端到端框架，直接从原始雷达数据回归人体参数，没有将信号解释与几何推理解耦，也未利用时间运动线索，限制了学习性能。为解决这一问题，我们提出了一种用于基于雷达的人体重建的两阶段框架。首先，我们引入人体反射提取模块，执行从粗到细的定位和体素级分割，生成一个置信度加权的雷达体积，编码体素级人体似然度。其次，我们设计了一个运动感知网格重建网络，通过双分支架构联合建模每帧几何和帧间动态来重建人体。大量实验表明，所提方法在保持计算效率的同时优于现有方法。

## CAST：用于手语识别的通道感知空间迁移学习与伪图像雷达

原英文标题：CAST: Channel-Aware Spatial Transfer Learning with Pseudo-Image Radar for Sign Language Recognition

作者：Md. Shakhoyat Rahman Shujon, Sheikh Md. Galib Mahim, Md. Milon Islam, Md Rezwanul Haque, Md Rabiul Islam, Hamdi Altaheri, Fakhri Karray

出版来源：arXiv

出版时间：2026-05-09

原英文摘要：We propose CAST, a dual-stream architecture that utilizes channel-aware spatial transfer learning for isolated sign language recognition addressing the challenges of magnitude-only 60~GHz radar Range-Time Maps (RTM). The proposed framework combines three physics-aware architectures with pretrained vision backbones, which operate under radar-only constraints across clinical and alphabetical gestures. First, an explicit decibel-to-linear inversion is combined with a windowed fast Fourier transform that extracts Cadence Velocity Diagrams (CVD) while avoiding the harmonic artifacts that arise from the spectral analysis of log-compressed signals. Second, a cross-antenna spatial attention module applies attention to raw antenna channels before the convolution, preserving inter-receiver amplitude covariance. Third, an asymmetric cross-attention mechanism fuses representations from parallel ConvNeXt-Tiny (CVD) and EfficientNetV2-S (RTM) backbones. Extensive experiments reveal that the architecture achieves a Top-1 accuracy of 80.5% under 5-fold cross-validation, establishing a 3.3% improvement over the best single-model baseline (77.2%). The findings suggest that physics-aware signal representations form a promising direction for radar-only sign language recognition under constrained sensor modalities. The source code is available at: https://github.com/Shakhoyat/CAST-at-SignEval2026.

中文摘要：我们提出CAST，一种双流架构，利用通道感知空间迁移学习进行孤立手语识别，解决了仅幅度的60 GHz雷达距离-时间图(RTM)带来的挑战。该框架将三种物理感知架构与预训练视觉主干结合，在仅使用雷达的约束下处理临床手势和字母手势。首先，显式的分贝到线性反演与加窗快速傅里叶变换相结合，提取节奏速度图(CVD)，同时避免了对对数压缩信号进行频谱分析时产生的谐波伪影。其次，跨天线空间注意力模块在卷积前对原始天线通道施加注意力，保留了接收器间的幅度协方差。第三，非对称交叉注意力机制融合来自并行ConvNeXt-Tiny (CVD)和EfficientNetV2-S (RTM)主干的特征表示。大量实验表明，该架构在5折交叉验证下达到80.5%的Top-1准确率，比最佳单模型基线(77.2%)提升3.3%。研究结果表明，物理感知信号表示为受限传感器模态下的纯雷达手语识别提供了一个有前景的方向。源代码可在：https://github.com/Shakhoyat/CAST-at-SignEval2026 获取。

In this article, we apply a large language model (LLM) to generate cloud description texts that include high-level semantic information, and we integrate cloud images, MMCR observations, and cloud description texts for cloud-type classification. ## 冲突触发的候选集优化：mmWave UAV系统中的贪婪扇区混合波束成形

原英文标题：Conflict-Triggered Candidate Refinement for Greedy Sector Hybrid Beamforming in mmWave UAV Systems

作者：Nam Van Dinh, Ngoc-Thanh Nguyen, Thuan Van Le

出版来源：2026 IEEE 15th International Conference on Communication Systems and Network Technologies (CSNT)

出版时间：2026-04-09

原英文摘要：Hybrid beamforming is a practical solution for mmWave UAV systems, where large antenna arrays are needed but fully digital processing is costly. In multi-user scenarios, greedy sector beam selection offers low complexity, yet its performance degrades in conflict-prone cases such as sector overlap, beam contention, and weak beam dominance. This paper proposes a two-stage hybrid beamforming framework with conflict-triggered candidate refinement. The transmitter first applies greedy sector beamforming as the default mode. A lightweight trigger then detects challenging channel realizations from beam-training indicators, and only in such cases activates a candidate refinement stage for analog beam selection, followed by digital RZF precoding. Simulation results show that the proposed method consistently improves the spectral efficiency of greedy sector beamforming while keeping the online complexity close to the greedy baseline. At 5 dB, the proposed scheme achieves an average gain of about $0.245 \text{bps} / \text{Hz}$ over all channel realizations and about $1.44 \text{bps} / \text{Hz}$ in triggered cases, while the trigger is activated in only about 17% of the realizations. The proposed framework also captures most of the gain of always-on refinement at low-to-moderate mobility, making it a practical complexity-aware beamforming strategy for mmWave UAV communications.

中文摘要：混合波束成形是mmWave UAV系统的一种实用解决方案，这类系统需要大型天线阵列，但全数字处理成本高昂。在多用户场景中，贪婪扇区波束选择复杂度低，但在易冲突的情况下（如扇区重叠、波束竞争和波束主导弱）性能会下降。本文提出了一种具有冲突触发候选集优化的两阶段混合波束成形框架。发射机首先将贪婪扇区波束成形作为默认模式。然后一个轻量级触发器从波束训练指标中检测具有挑战性的信道实现，并仅在此类情况下激活用于模拟波束选择的候选集优化阶段，随后进行数字RZF预编码。仿真结果表明，所提方法持续提高了贪婪扇区波束成形的频谱效率，同时保持在线复杂度接近贪婪基线。在5 dB时，所提方案在所有信道实现上的平均增益约为$0.245 \text{bps} / \text{Hz}$，在触发情况下约为$1.44 \text{bps} / \text{Hz}$，而触发器仅在约17%的实现中被激活。所提框架还捕获了低到中等移动性下常开优化的大部分增益，使其成为mmWave UAV通信中一种实用的复杂度感知波束成形策略。

## 利用消费级毫米波雷达的遮挡非接触材料特性表征

原英文标题：Contactless Material Characterization Through Occlusion Utilizing Consumer-Grade mmWave Radar

作者：Ryder Swan

出版来源：Master of Science thesis, University of West Florida

出版时间：2026-05

原英文摘要：As robotic systems and autonomous environments require increasingly robust perception capabilities, traditional optical sensors often fail in non-line-of-sight or occluded conditions. While millimeter-wave (mmWave) radar offers a penetrative alternative, the boundary limits of low-cost, consumer-grade mmWave systems for classifying object materials through secondary physical masks remain largely undefined. This thesis investigates the operational capabilities and physical limits of a Texas Instruments IWR6843ISK Frequency Modulated Continuous Wave (FMCW) radar in detecting and categorizing concealed objects. To isolate physical limitations, the sensor's radio frequency (RF) transmission parameters were held constant while environmental variables including masking material density, mask thickness, object distance, and dataset size were systematically manipulated across six testing phases. Captured multi-antenna radar reflections were processed as standardized time-domain signals and evaluated using neural network classifiers. The results demonstrate the system's high baseline accuracy in classifying metallic and non-metallic targets through varied occlusions, including fabric, styrofoam, and wood. However, systematic limit testing revealed distinct operational thresholds, showing significant signal attenuation and subsequent classification degradation as the masking material's density and thickness increased. Additionally, a comparative evaluation of deep learning architectures demonstrated that while a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) achieved the highest overall accuracy, a Deep Multi-Layer Perceptron (MLP) offered highly competitive performance with vastly superior computational efficiency and ultra-low inference latency. Ultimately, this research validates the feasibility of utilizing consumer-grade mmWave radar as a non-invasive, contactless alternative to traditional induction-based metal detectors, while successfully defining both the physical and computational constraints necessary for its practical implementation.

原英文摘要：Millimeter-wave (mmWave) radar provides privacy-preserving sensing and is valuable for human action recognition (HAR). Existing mmWave point cloud datasets are limited in scale and mostly collected under homogeneous single-source settings, preventing current methods from handling real-world distribution shifts caused by heterogeneous radar sources, such as different devices and frequency bands. To address this, we introduce UniMM-HAR, the largest and first mmWave point cloud HAR dataset for heterogeneous multi-source scenarios, standardizing three distinct radar configurations to realistically evaluate cross-source generalization. Leveraging action-consistent spatio-temporal Doppler patterns as anchors, the Dual-space Doppler Reparameterization (D2R) module performs sample-adaptive geometric densification and Doppler-guided feature recalibration, while the Text Alignment Module (TAM) provides stable semantic anchors via a pretrained textual space. Experiments show that DAP-Net significantly outperforms existing methods under heterogeneous radar settings, achieving state-of-the-art accuracy and strong cross-source robustness.
