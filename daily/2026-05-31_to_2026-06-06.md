## 一种黑暗鲁棒的美国手语字母识别方法：基于深度学习与FMCW uRAD传感 [M]

原英文标题：A Darkness-Resilient Approach to ASL Alphabet Recognition Using Deep Learning and FMCW uRAD Sensing

作者：Fatma Makhlouf, Fethi Mejri, Neji Youssef

出版来源：Lecture Notes on Data Engineering and Communications Technologies

出版时间：2026-05-29

原英文摘要：Communication serves as a fundamental link among individuals globally; without this medium of engagement, people risk losing their sense of purpose in life. In particular, communication poses a challenge for hearing individuals and those who are deaf or unable to hear. Many existing systems and applications have demonstrated their ability to recognize the sign language of deaf or mute people. However, these applications are still useful in standard or basic situations but not in critical scenarios, such as at night. This type of case remains underexplored and requires critical consideration. Although many studies based on images are affected by the luminosity of the captors, the sensitivity of gesture images requires a high level of preprocessing before they can be classified, and certain significant features may disappear after deep treatment. In this context, we propose a study to build an application that can classify gestures under various conditions, such as at night or in the dark. As a second objective, we needed to validate whether the variation in data affected the accuracy of gesture recognition. This paper presents a novel method for addressing limitations related to low-quality resolution images by recognizing five ASL gestures in the dark using raw data from FMCW radar and a convolutional neural network (CNN) after collecting them in various orientations or angles to analyze the impact of dataset variability on the model.

## SISO FSA-FMCW雷达基于测量驱动的高精度二维定位框架 [J]

原英文标题：A Measurement-Driven Framework of SISO FSA-FMCW Radar for High-Accuracy 2-D Localization

作者：Sijie Chen, You Lv, Xiaoying Zheng, Haojun Ge, Xiaonan Hui, Xianmin Zhang, Shilie Zheng

出版来源：IEEE Transactions on Microwave Theory and Techniques

出版时间：2026-06-01

原英文摘要：Frequency-scanned antenna (FSA)-based frequency-modulated continuous-wave (FMCW) radars provide a compact and hardware-efficient solution for 2-D localization by encoding both range and angle information into a single frequency sweep. However, practical FSA-FMCW systems often suffer from limited angular scanning caused by antenna open-stopband (OSB) effects and degraded direction-of-arrival (DOA) estimation accuracy due to the mismatch between realized antenna responses and idealized models. To address these limitations, this article presents a measurement-driven framework for practical single-input single-output (SISO) FSA-FMCW localization. An OSB-suppressed substrate-integrated waveguide (SIW) leaky-wave antenna (LWA) is employed to provide continuous frequency scanning over a wide angular range from −50° to 35° across 8–11 GHz. Based on measured far-field data, the complex equivalent array response is reconstructed under physical dispersion constraints, enabling signal-subspace-projection (SSP)-based DOA estimation that is robust to antenna-response mismatch and practical nonidealities. Outdoor experiments with the implemented SISO FSA-FMCW radar demonstrate high-accuracy 2-D localization, achieving an angular RMSE of 1.3° and a range RMSE of 11 cm. These results verify the effectiveness of the proposed framework for compact radar sensing platforms requiring accurate localization with low RF complexity.

中文摘要：基于频率扫描天线（FSA）的调频连续波（FMCW）雷达通过将距离和角度信息编码到单次频率扫描中，提供了一种紧凑且硬件高效的二维定位解决方案。然而，实际FSA-FMCW系统常因天线开路阻带（OSB）效应导致角度扫描范围受限，并因实际天线响应与理想模型之间的失配导致到达角（DOA）估计精度下降。为解决这些问题，本文提出了一种面向实际单输入单输出（SISO）FSA-FMCW定位的测量驱动框架。采用抑制OSB的基片集成波导（SIW）漏波天线（LWA），在8–11 GHz频段内实现从−50°到35°的宽角连续扫描。基于实测远场数据，在物理色散约束下重建复等效阵列响应，从而实现基于信号子空间投影（SSP）的DOA估计，该方法对天线响应失配和实际非理想特性具有鲁棒性。利用所实现的SISO FSA-FMCW雷达进行的室外实验展示了高精度二维定位，角度RMSE达到1.3°，距离RMSE为11 cm。这些结果验证了所提框架在需要低射频复杂度且高精度定位的紧凑型雷达传感平台中的有效性。

## 一种新型毫米波圆极化SIW缝隙天线阵列 [M]

原英文标题：A Novel Millimeter‐Wave Circularly‐Polarized SIW Slot Antenna Array

作者：Zi‐Lin Sun, Run‐Kuan Liu, Rui‐Sen Chen, Shao‐Ting Mao, Qiang Shao, Guan‐Long Huang, Xian‐Hui He, Mustafa K. Taher Al‐Nuaimi, Sai‐Wai Wong

出版来源：Microwave and Optical Technology Letters

出版时间：2026-05-28

原英文摘要：ABSTRACT Millimeter‐wave circularly‐polarized substrate integrated waveguide (SIW) slot antennas using novel resonant modes are proposed in this paper. Unlike conventional circularly polarized SIW slot antennas, the proposed circularly polarized slot antenna employs two novel SIW TE 101 and TE 011 cavity modes, with the electric field oriented parallel to the metal plane. The resonant frequencies of TE 101 and TE 011 modes in SIW cavity are mainly determined by the cavity height. Consequently, they have high resonant frequencies, demonstrating good feasibility for designing millimeter‐wave circularly‐polarized slot antennas. Additionally, the radiation slots on the top metal plane are excited by the electric field of the cavity modes, which eliminates the need of power dividing network for feeding the array elements, thereby simplifying the antenna feeding structure. A 4×4 millimeter‐wave circularly polarized SIW slot array is then designed and analyzed. The final results show that the proposed antenna can achieve 3.24% operation bandwidth, 12.19 dBic peak gain, and higher than 90% total efficiency.

中文摘要：摘要：本文提出了一种利用新型谐振模式的毫米波圆极化衬底集成波导（SIW）缝隙天线。与传统圆极化SIW缝隙天线不同，所提出的圆极化缝隙天线采用了两种新型SIW TE101和TE011腔模，电场方向平行于金属平面。SIW腔中TE101和TE011模式的谐振频率主要由腔体高度决定。因此，它们具有高谐振频率，为设计毫米波圆极化缝隙天线展现了良好的可行性。此外，顶部金属平面上的辐射缝隙由腔模的电场激励，这消除了为阵列单元馈电所需的功分网络，从而简化了天线馈电结构。随后设计并分析了一个4×4毫米波圆极化SIW缝隙阵列。最终结果表明，所提出的天线可实现3.24%的工作带宽、12.19 dBic的峰值增益以及高于90%的总效率。

## AE-CFAR多目标检测：用于24GHz级联汽车毫米波雷达 [Preprint]

原英文标题：AE-CFAR Multi-Target Detection for 24GHz Cascaded Automotive mmWave Radar

作者：YiKui He, Ralph~Gerard~B. Sangalang, Anton~Louise~P. Ocampo, Bin Li

出版来源：Research Square

出版时间：2026-05-28

原英文摘要：This paper addresses the need for multi-target detection using automotive mmWave radar in complex traffic scenarios and proposes a 24 GHz cascaded system and an AE-CFAR detection link in the RDM domain. The system employs SRK2401 + SRK2402 to construct a 3T8R cascaded transceiver front-end, with 8Rx parallel IF sampling on the receiving side to form a data cube. On the end side, STM32H753 is responsible for front-end configuration, timing scheduling, and data transfer, while TMS320C6748 performs range FFT, velocity FFT to construct the RDM, and executes AE-CFAR detection. The algorithm suppresses training window contamination through truncated mean robust noise estimation in the RDM domain, combines edge-aware small-side selection in the 1 range dimension for adaptive threshold correction, and utilizes 3T8R observation redundancy for cross-channel/cross-slot consistency screening to reduce false alarms caused by mutual interference pseudo-peaks and occasional noise. Simulation, anechoic chamber, and real-vehicle test results show that the system covers a detection range of 6–140 m and a speed measurement range of 14–225 km/h, with a range resolution of ±5 cm and a velocity resolution of ±0.7 km/h, supporting simultaneous tracking of up to 50 dynamic targets. In comprehensive scenarios, the false alarm rate is reduced by approximately 20%, the detection accuracy is improved by about 15%, the miss rate in multi-target working conditions at 150 m is reduced by more than 10%, and the end-side processing delay does not exceed 5 ms.

## AFDM–FMCW波形：通过DAFT核线性调频嵌入的低复杂度集成感知与通信 [C]

作者：Salman Ahmad, Soo Young Shin

出版来源：Proceedings of Symposium of the Korean Institute of Communications and Information Sciences (KICS) Winter Conference

出版时间：2026-02-04

原英文摘要：Integrated sensing and communication (ISAC) must balance sensing accuracy, communication reliability, and receiver complexity. Frequency-modulated continuous-wave (FMCW) radar enables low-complexity analog de-chirping and accurate range-Doppler sensing, while multicarrier waveforms primarily target high-rate links. This paper proposes an affine frequency division multiplexing (AFDM)-FMCW waveform that generates an FMCW-like chirp from selected entries of the inverse discrete affine Fourier transform (DAFT) kernel and preserves chirp continuity under cyclic prefix (CP) insertion. The resulting signal maintains an AFDM communication structure while providing a continuous chirp for analog mixing at the sensing receiver. A unified transmitter/receiver model is presented, supporting DAFT-based demodulation for communications and 2-D FFT range-Doppler processing for sensing. The sensing-communication trade-off is controlled by the chirp-to-data power ratio.

Millimeter-wave (mmWave) radar offers motion-aware and geometry-consistent priors that hold significant potential for mitigating such artifacts. Extensive experiments on real-world datasets demonstrate that Co-D 2 achieves superior performance in both detection and artifact suppression, highlighting its effectiveness for robust multimodal perception in complex environments.

## 相干雷达网络：术语、同步与应用 [J]

出版来源：IEEE Journal of Microwaves

出版时间：2026-05-27

原英文摘要：In coherent radar networks, multiple radar sensors not only operate cooperatively but also facilitate coherent evaluation of signals from other spacially distant radar units. This overcomes the limitations of single radars, enabling extended apertures and new multistatic sensing geometries. Applications are manifold, ranging from network-based direction of arrival (DoA) estimation to bi-/multistatic synthetic aperture radar (SAR) and joint communication and sensing (JCAS). In recent years, this has led to significant research interest and the design of a wide range of radar networks and synchronization concepts. This paper introduces a unified terminology and a general signal model applicable to radar networks and bistatic radars. Building on this model, this work provides a detailed review of the main sources of synchronization errors and their impact on radar signal processing. The paper further surveys and categorizes synchronization strategies proposed in the literature to address these errors.
