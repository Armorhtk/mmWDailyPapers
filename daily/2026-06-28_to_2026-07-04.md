## 紧凑型MIMO FMCW雷达用于多人定位与生命体征分离检测 [J]

原英文标题：A compact MIMO FMCW radar for locating multiple people and separate detection of vital signs

作者：Tatsunori Saito, Takeshi Toda

出版来源：IEICE Transactions on Communications

出版时间：2026-06-22

原英文摘要：In recent years, radar-based remote and non-contact vital sign detection has garnered significant attention. However, multiple subjects at the same distance and range bin overlap caused by walking can induce phase interference, making it one of the most challenging issues in non contact vital sign detection. This problem can hinder vital sign detection and significantly degrade accuracy. Furthermore, most studies assume that subjects remain stationary as a prerequisite for measurement, which limits the feasibility and applicability of radar-based vital sign monitoring systems in real-world scenarios. Therefore, in this study, we proposed the application of a compact multiple-input multiple-output millimeter-wave frequency-modulated continuous wave radar for multi-person localization and heart rate estimation. A point cloud-based signal processing algorithm utilizing minimum variance distortionless response beamforming was designed to separate vital signs from stationary and moving subjects. The experiment conducted simultaneous heart rate measurements for multiple subjects across five experimental scenarios, achieving an average accuracy of 93.51%. Furthermore, this study evaluated heart rate estimation in real world-like conditions, such as range bin overlap, demonstrating that the proposed method is capable of accurately measuring heart rates across various scenarios.

中文摘要：近年来，基于雷达的远程非接触式生命体征检测引起了广泛关注。然而，同一距离上的多个目标以及行走引起的距离单元重叠会导致相位干扰，这成为非接触式生命体征检测中最具挑战性的问题之一。该问题会阻碍生命体征检测并显著降低准确性。此外，大多数研究将目标保持静止作为测量前提，这限制了基于雷达的生命体征监测系统在真实场景中的可行性和适用性。因此，本研究提出应用紧凑型多输入多输出毫米波调频连续波（MIMO FMCW）雷达进行多人定位与心率估计。设计了一种基于点云的最小方差无失真响应（MVDR）波束成形信号处理算法，以分离静止和移动目标的生命体征。实验在五个场景中同时测量多个目标的心率，平均准确率达到93.51%。此外，本研究在类似真实世界的条件下（如距离单元重叠）评估了心率估计，证明所提方法能够在各种场景下准确测量心率。

## 基于60 GHz FMCW雷达的非接触式边缘AI原型：用于模拟呼吸抑制和运动伪影检测 [M]

原英文标题：A Contactless Edge-AI Prototype for Simulated Apnea-like Respiratory Suppression and Motion Artifact Detection Using 60 GHz FMCW Radar

作者：Sathit Pairoch, Pattarapong Phasukkit, Nongluck Houngkamhang

出版来源：Technologies

出版时间：2026-06-24

原英文摘要：Sleep-related respiratory disturbances are difficult to monitor continuously outside specialized laboratories because conventional polysomnography is resource-intensive and intrusive. This study presents a contactless edge-AI engineering prototype for detecting controlled voluntary respiratory-motion suppression and motion artifacts using a 60 GHz frequency-modulated continuous-wave radar. The system integrates a 60 GHz radar front end, lightweight local preprocessing, an INT8 one-dimensional convolutional neural network deployed on the Analog Devices MAX78000 CNN accelerator (Analog Devices Thailand, Chon Buri, Thailand), and an event-driven Raspberry Pi Zero 2W gateway for alert transmission. Evaluation was performed using a controlled healthy-volunteer dataset consisting of normal breathing, voluntary breath-holding-induced respiratory suppression, and deliberate motion artifact. The final valid test set contained 270 technically valid 30 s windows balanced across the three classes. The INT8 model achieved an overall accuracy of 92.6% (95% confidence interval: 88.8–95.2%), with a macro-averaged precision, recall, and F1-score of 92.6%, 92.6%, and 92.5%, respectively. Active CNN inference on the MAX78000 consumed 0.152 ± 0.011 mJ and was completed in 5.20 ± 0.11 ms, corresponding to approximately 280-fold lower active inference energy than Python 3.14.6/TensorFlow Lite 2.21.0-based execution on the Raspberry Pi Zero 2W. These results demonstrate the feasibility of privacy-aware, low-power respiratory-pattern classification at the edge. However, the study should be interpreted strictly as an engineering proof-of-concept based on controlled voluntary breathing and movement tasks in healthy volunteers. It is not a clinically validated apnea or obstructive sleep apnea detection system and did not include polysomnography, oxygen saturation measurement, airflow sensing, sleep staging, or diagnosed patient cohorts.

## 两阶段运动感知框架：基于毫米波雷达的人体网格恢复 [Preprint]

原英文标题：A Two-Stage Motion-Aware Framework for mmWave-based Human Mesh Recovery

作者：Hoang Hai Pham, Shuntian Zheng, Jiaqi Li, Yu Guan

出版来源：arXiv

出版时间：2026-05-08

原英文摘要：Millimeter-wave (mmWave) radar has emerged as a promising sensing modality for human perception due to its robustness under challenging environmental conditions and strong privacy-preserving properties. However, recovering accurate 3D human body meshes from radar observations remains difficult due to severe signal clutter and the inherently partial nature of radar measurements. Previous works typically adopt end-to-end frameworks that directly regress human body parameters from raw radar data, without decoupling signal interpretation from geometric reasoning or exploiting temporal motion cues, limiting learning performance. To address this, we propose a two-stage framework for radar-based human body reconstruction. First, we introduce a human reflection extraction module that performs coarse-to-fine localization and voxel-wise segmentation to produce a confidence-weighted radar volume encoding voxel-level human likelihood. Second, we design a motion-aware mesh recovery network that reconstructs the human body by jointly modeling per-frame geometry and inter-frame dynamics using a dual-branch architecture. Extensive experiments demonstrate that the proposed method outperforms existing approaches while maintaining computational efficiency.

中文摘要：毫米波雷达因其在恶劣环境条件下的鲁棒性和强隐私保护特性，已成为一种有前景的人体感知传感模态。然而，由于严重的信号杂波和雷达测量固有的部分性，从雷达观测中恢复准确的3D人体网格仍然困难。以往的工作通常采用端到端框架，直接从原始雷达数据回归人体参数，而没有将信号解释与几何推理分离，也没有利用时间运动线索，限制了学习性能。为了解决这一问题，我们提出了一个基于雷达的人体重建两阶段框架。首先，我们引入一个人体反射提取模块，执行从粗到细的定位和体素级分割，生成一个置信度加权的雷达体积，编码体素级人体似然。其次，我们设计了一个运动感知网格恢复网络，通过双分支架构联合建模每帧几何形状和帧间动态来重建人体。大量实验表明，所提出的方法在保持计算效率的同时优于现有方法。

## 基于FMCW雷达的非接触式监测生命体征检测方法 [J]

原英文标题：A Vital Signs Detection Method for Contactless Monitoring Using FMCW Radar

作者：Yansheng Zhu, Yong Wang, Langjuan Luo, Liangbo Xie, Xiaolong Yang

出版来源：IEEE Sensors Journal

出版时间：2026-06-24

原英文摘要：In recent years, non-contact vital sign monitoring has garnered widespread attention. This paper proposes a vital signs monitoring method using a millimeter-wave Multiple-Input Multiple-Output (MIMO) Frequency Modulated Continuous Wave (FMCW) radar. The method leverages the MIMO capability of the measurement system’s antennas to combine the received signals using the Maximum Ratio Combining (MRC) method. Then, the Adaptive Amplitude Adjustment (AdAA) technique is applied to remove the interference that caused by body movement and harmonic interference, while the Fixed Bandwidth Filtering (FBF) method is presented to eliminate the clutter interference. To mitigate the randomness of the FastICA algorithm during decomposition, an improved FastICA algorithm is proposed. It calculates the Pearson correlation coefficient between the high-frequency heartbeat signal separated by conventional FastICA and the phase-combined signal. The separation result whose coefficient meets a predefined threshold is then taken as the quasi-vital sign signal. The quasi-respiration signal is obtained via bandpass filtering and spectral analysis, while the quasi-heartbeat signal is acquired by bandpass filtering combined with autocorrelation. Experimental results verify that the proposed method outperforms existing approaches.

中文摘要：近年来，非接触式生命体征监测已引起广泛关注。本文提出了一种利用毫米波多输入多输出（MIMO）调频连续波（FMCW）雷达的生命体征监测方法。该方法利用测量系统天线的MIMO能力，采用最大比合并（MRC）方法对接收信号进行合并。然后，应用自适应幅度调整（AdAA）技术去除由身体运动引起的干扰和谐波干扰，同时提出固定带宽滤波（FBF）方法以消除杂波干扰。为减轻FastICA算法在分解过程中的随机性，提出了一种改进的FastICA算法：计算传统FastICA分离出的高频心跳信号与相位合并信号之间的皮尔逊相关系数，将相关系数满足预设阈值的分离结果作为准生命体征信号。通过带通滤波和频谱分析获得准呼吸信号，而准心跳信号则通过带通滤波结合自相关获得。实验结果验证了所提方法优于现有方法。

## ActiveVital：面向家庭医疗保健机器人的几何感知体感生命体征监测 [Preprint]

原英文标题：ActiveVital: Geometry-Aware Embodied Vital Signs Monitoring for Home Healthcare Robots

作者：Yuxuan Hu, Shihao Li, Yang Xiao, Gen Li, Feng Xu, Jianfei Yang

出版来源：arXiv

出版时间：2026-06-29

原英文摘要：Home robots require reliable vital signs monitoring to support long-term companionship and safety in daily environments, yet obtaining respiration and heart rate without physical contact remains challenging in unconstrained home settings. Millimeter-wave (mmWave) radar offers a promising solution due to its phase sensitivity to sub-millimeter motions. However, mmWave measurements are fundamentally constrained by observation geometry, since only the radial component of motion is observable. Consequently, arbitrary robot-human orientations often introduce angular misalignment that destabilizes vital signs estimation. To address this limitation, we reformulate vital signs monitoring from passive signal recovery to active geometric regulation. We propose ActiveVital, a vision-guided sensing framework that treats sensing geometry as an explicit control variable for robots. It localizes the chest anchor via visual keypoints and converts alignment errors into control commands. This steers the robot-mounted radar toward near-normal incidence to the thoracic surface, maximizing radial observability within a perception-action loop. A differential phase enhancement module further stabilizes signal extraction under motion. Experiments show that ActiveVital reduces respiration interval error from 0.87 s to 0.14 s and heart rate error from 13.59 bpm to 2.22 bpm, achieving accuracy comparable to controlled static sensing while remaining robust under unconstrained robot-human configurations.

中文摘要：家庭机器人需要可靠的生命体征监测以支持日常环境中的长期陪伴和安全，但在无约束的家庭环境中，无需物理接触即可获取呼吸和心率仍然具有挑战性。毫米波(mmWave)雷达因其对亚毫米运动的相位灵敏度而提供了一种有前景的解决方案。然而，毫米波测量从根本上受限于观测几何结构，因为只有运动的径向分量是可观测的。因此，任意的机器人-人体朝向经常引入角度错位，使生命体征估计不稳定。为了解决这一限制，我们将生命体征监测从被动信号恢复重新构建为主动几何调控。我们提出ActiveVital，一个视觉引导的感知框架，将感知几何作为机器人的显式控制变量。它通过视觉关键点定位胸部锚点，并将对齐误差转换为控制命令。这在感知-动作循环中引导安装在机器人上的雷达接近法向入射到胸腔表面，最大化径向可观测性。一个差分相位增强模块进一步稳定运动下的信号提取。实验表明，ActiveVital将呼吸间隔误差从0.87秒降低到0.14秒，心率误差从13.59 bpm降低到2.22 bpm，实现了与受控静态感知相当的精度，同时在无约束的机器人-人体配置下保持鲁棒性。

Meanwhile, the improved network further reduces the model complexity and computational cost while maintaining a lightweight structure, making it valuable for engineering applications such as automotive radar.

## 阴影无疑：利用FMCW雷达阴影效应进行近距离几何重建 [Preprint]

出版来源：arXiv

出版时间：2026-06-24

原英文摘要：Reliable perception in adverse conditions remains challenging for autonomous systems, as cameras and LiDAR degrade in poor lighting or weather. Millimetre-wave FMCW radar is robust to such conditions, but its elevation collapse limits geometric reasoning. We observe that vehicle chassis occlude radar rays and form a distinctive geometric shadow, and its consistency can enable us to infer useful information about objects whose returns intersect this shadow. 原英文摘要：Wireless world models aim to represent, predict, and reason about wireless propagation by jointly understanding physical environments and channel responses. Realizing such models in sixth-generation (6G) digital twin channels requires datasets that capture measured wireless responses and environment states under real-world propagation conditions. This paper presents WiWorld-RealData, a real-world outdoor multi-band channel and multi-modal sensing dataset collected along campus mobile routes. WiWorld-RealData provides measured channel impulse responses (CIRs) at 3.7 GHz and 6.775 GHz, together with multi-view images, panoramic images, light detection and ranging (LiDAR) point clouds, millimeter-wave (mmWave) radar records, and global navigation satellite system (GNSS) trajectories. Through unified file organization and metadata manifests, the dataset establishes sample-level correspondences among channel responses, environment observations, timestamps, route information, antenna configurations, and quality flags. The overall measurement campaign has produced 10 TB-level multi-modal field data. The current public release provides one representative dual-band route at 3.7 GHz and 6.775 GHz with complete channel-environment alignment, while the acquisition framework supports extension to more frequency bands and scenarios. A case study on environment-assisted path-loss prediction achieves a mean absolute error (MAE) of 2.02 dB and a root mean squared error (RMSE) of 2.69 dB, indicating that the aligned environment observations contain predictive information for channel variations. The dataset is available at https://scc.bupt.edu.cn/dataset-manage/datasets/44, and a ScienceDB mirror will be provided upon release.

中文摘要：无线世界模型旨在通过联合理解物理环境和信道响应，来表示、预测和推理无线传播。在第六代（6G）数字孪生信道中实现此类模型，需要捕获在真实世界传播条件下测量的无线响应和环境状态的数据集。本文介绍了WiWorld-RealData，这是一个沿校园移动路线收集的真实世界室外多频段信道与多模态感知数据集。WiWorld-RealData提供了在3.7 GHz和6.775 GHz下测量的信道冲激响应（CIR），以及多视角图像、全景图像、光探测与测距（LiDAR）点云、毫米波（mmWave）雷达记录和全球导航卫星系统（GNSS）轨迹。通过统一文件组织和元数据清单，该数据集在信道响应、环境观测、时间戳、路径信息、天线配置和质量标志之间建立了样本级对应关系。整体测量活动已产生10 TB级别的多模态现场数据。当前公开发布提供了一个在3.7 GHz和6.775 GHz下具有完整信道-环境对齐的代表性双频段路线，而采集框架支持扩展到更多频段和场景。一项关于环境辅助路径损耗预测的案例研究实现了2.02 dB的平均绝对误差（MAE）和2.69 dB的均方根误差（RMSE），表明对齐的环境观测包含信道变化的预测性信息。该数据集可从https://scc.bupt.edu.cn/dataset-manage/datasets/44获取，发布后将提供ScienceDB镜像。
