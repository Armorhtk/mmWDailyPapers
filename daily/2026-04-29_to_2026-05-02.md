## CLLAP：基于对比学习的激光雷达增强预训练以改进雷达-相机融合

原英文标题：CLLAP: Contrastive Learning-based LiDAR-Augmented Pretraining for Enhanced Radar-Camera Fusion

作者：Bingyi Liu, Chuanhui Zhu, Hongfei Xue, Jian Teng, Jipeng Liu, Enshu Wang, Penglin Dai, Pu Wang

出版来源：arXiv

出版时间：2026-04-27

原英文摘要：Accurate 3D object detection is critical for autonomous driving, necessitating reliable, cost-effective sensors capable of operating in adverse weather conditions. Camera and millimeter-wave radar fusion has emerged as a promising solution; however, these methods often rely on finely annotated radar data, which is scarce and labor-intensive to produce. To address this challenge, we present CLLAP, a Contrastive Learning-based LiDAR-Augmented Pretraining framework that enhances the performance of existing radar-camera fusion methods for 3D object detection. CLLAP leverages abundant LiDAR data to generate pseudo-radar data using the proposed L2R (LiDAR-to-Radar) Sampling method. Then, it incorporates this data into a novel dual-stage, dual-modality contrastive learning strategy, enabling effective self-supervised learning from paired pseudo-radar and image data. This approach facilitates effective pretraining of existing radar-camera fusion models in a plug-and-play manner, enhancing their feature extraction capabilities and improving detection accuracy and robustness. Experimental results using NuScenes and Lyft Level 5 datasets demonstrate significant performance improvements across three baseline models, highlighting CLLAP's effectiveness in advancing radar-camera fusion for autonomous driving applications.

中文摘要：精确的3D目标检测对于自动驾驶至关重要，这需要能够在恶劣天气条件下工作的可靠且成本效益高的传感器。相机与毫米波雷达融合已成为一种有前景的解决方案；然而，这些方法通常依赖于精细标注的雷达数据，而此类数据稀缺且标注劳动密集。为应对这一挑战，我们提出CLLAP，一个基于对比学习的激光雷达增强预训练框架，能够提升现有雷达-相机融合方法在3D目标检测中的性能。CLLAP利用丰富的LiDAR数据，通过所提出的L2R（LiDAR-to-Radar）采样方法生成伪雷达数据。然后，将这些数据融入一种新颖的双阶段、双模态对比学习策略，实现从成对伪雷达和图像数据中有效的自监督学习。该方法以即插即用的方式对现有雷达-相机融合模型进行高效预训练，增强其特征提取能力，并提高检测精度与鲁棒性。在NuScenes和Lyft Level 5数据集上的实验结果表明，三个基线模型的性能均有显著提升，彰显了CLLAP在推进自动驾驶中雷达-相机融合方面的有效性。

## 基于深度辅助学习的射频信号无接触室性早搏诊断

原英文标题：Contactless Premature Ventricular Contractions Diagnosis with Radio Frequency Signals based on Deep Auxiliary Learning

作者：Xilong Yuan, Bin-Bin Zhang, Zehan Guo, Yang Hu, Yan Chen

出版来源：IEEE Internet of Things Journal

出版时间：2026-04-27

原英文摘要：Cardiovascular diseases dominate global mortality, with premature ventricular contractions (PVCs) among the most prevalent arrhythmias. PVCs are paroxysmal and sporadic. If undetected, they may escalate to lethal arrhythmias or sudden cardiac death, underscoring the imperative for early and accurate diagnosis. Conventional monitoring via electrocardiogram (ECG) necessitates prolonged skin-electrode contact, provoking discomfort and potential allergic reactions, whereas photoplethysmography (PPG) is intrinsically susceptible to ambient light and motion artifacts. These limitations restrict both modalities to short-term or intermittent use. Radio-frequency (RF) sensing offers a contactless alternative for unobtrusive, long-term cardiac surveillance. The cardiac-induced mechanical displacements, however, are orders of magnitude smaller than respiratory and body-motion artifacts, rendering PVC-related signatures in RF echoes highly susceptible to noise and consequently difficult to extract and classify. To handle this, we propose a PVC diagnosis model based on RF signals, which constructs an auxiliary learning framework, thereby improving the accuracy of PVC diagnosis. To further enhance the effectiveness of the auxiliary framework, we designed a residual-assisted Mixture of Experts architecture, which effectively alleviates the gradient conflict between the auxiliary task and the main task. We conducted experiments on a large-scale dataset (7,015 subjects) in a hospital outpatient setting, where our system achieved the best performance, demonstrating the superiority of our approach.

中文摘要：心血管疾病是全球致死主因，其中室性早搏（PVCs）是最常见的心律失常之一。PVCs呈阵发性和偶发性，若未被检测到，可能恶化为致命性心律失常或心源性猝死，因此早期准确诊断至关重要。传统的心电图监测需要长时间皮肤-电极接触，引起不适和潜在过敏反应，而光电容积描记法（PPG）本身易受环境光和运动伪影影响。这些局限使得两种方式仅适用于短期或间歇性使用。射频（RF）感知提供了一种无接触替代方案，用于非侵入式、长期的心脏监测。然而，心脏引起的机械位移比呼吸和身体运动伪影小几个数量级，导致RF回波中与PVC相关的信号极易受噪声影响，难以提取和分类。为此，我们提出了一种基于RF信号的PVC诊断模型，构建了辅助学习框架，从而提高了PVC诊断的准确性。为进一步增强辅助框架的有效性，我们设计了一种残差辅助的专家混合架构，有效缓解了辅助任务与主任务之间的梯度冲突。我们在医院门诊环境中对大规模数据集（7,015名受试者）进行了实验，系统取得了最佳性能，证明了我们方法的优越性。

## 控制你的查询：用于相机-雷达成像的异构查询交互

原英文标题：Control Your Queries: Heterogeneous Query Interaction for Camera-Radar Fusion

作者：Jialong Wu, Yihan Wang, Matthias Rottmann

出版来源：arXiv

出版时间：2026-04-28

原英文摘要：In autonomous driving, camera-radar fusion offers complementary sensing and low deployment cost. Existing methods perform fusion through input mixing, feature map mixing, or query-based feature sampling. We propose a new fusion paradigm, termed heterogeneous query interaction, and present ConFusion, a camera-radar 3D object detector. ConFusion combines image queries, radar queries, and learnable world queries distributed in 3D space to improve query initialization and object coverage. To encourage cross-type interaction among heterogeneous queries, we introduce heterogeneous query mixing (QMix), which performs dedicated cross-type attention after feature sampling to consolidate complementary object evidence. We further propose interactive query swap sampling (QSwap), which improves feature sampling by allowing related queries to exchange informative feature tokens under attention and geometric constraints. Experiments on the nuScenes dataset show that ConFusion achieves state-of-the-art performance, reaching 59.1 mAP and 65.6 NDS on the validation set, and 61.6 mAP and 67.9 NDS on the test set.

中文摘要：在自动驾驶中，相机-雷达融合提供了互补感知和低部署成本。现有方法通过输入混合、特征图混合或基于查询的特征采样进行融合。我们提出了一种新的融合范式，称为异构查询交互，并提出了ConFusion，一个相机-雷达3D物体检测器。ConFusion结合了图像查询、雷达查询和分布在3D空间中的可学习世界查询，以改进查询初始化和物体覆盖。为鼓励异构查询之间的跨类型交互，我们引入了异构查询混合（QMix），它在特征采样后进行专用跨类型注意力，以整合互补的物体证据。我们进一步提出了交互式查询交换采样（QSwap），它通过允许相关查询在注意力和几何约束下交换信息特征token来改进特征采样。在nuScenes数据集上的实验表明，ConFusion达到了最先进的性能，在验证集上达到59.1 mAP和65.6 NDS，在测试集上达到61.6 mAP和67.9 NDS。

## CoSense: 基于WiFi CSI和毫米波雷达的呼吸检测

原英文标题：CoSense: Respiratory Detection Based on WIFI CSI and Millimeter -Wave Radar

作者：J Liu, X Qiao, Z Wu, W Fan, Y Zuo, X He

出版来源：2026 29th International Conference on Computer Supported Cooperative Work in Design (CSCWD 2026)

出版时间：2026-05-02

原英文摘要：—In complex indoor environments, single-mode wireless respiration detection is susceptible to multipath interference and noise interference, leading to a decrease in accuracy. To address this limitation, this paper proposes CoSense, a contactless respiration detection system based on WiFi-millimeter wave joint sensing. CoSense fuses weighted signal features to enhance sensing accuracy. First, this paper constructs a CSI and FMCW sensing model for complex indoor environments and derives the mapping relationship between human respiration and signal changes. Based on this, the WiFi branch constructs a covariance matrix from multi-antenna, multi-subcarrier CSI and extracts the principal feature direction to achieve adaptive merging and noise suppression of respiration-related dynamic components; The millimeter-wave branch performs a two-dimensional Fourier transform, pooling low-frequency Doppler energy at the target range gate to estimate the respiratory rate. This paper further designs a physically consistent multi-mode fusion strategy and constructs a comprehensive quality factor based on the individual confidence-to-noise ratios, using a weighted approach to obtain the final respiratory frequency estimate. Experiments show that CoSense achieves higher detection accuracy compared to existing methods, providing a more accurate and effective feasible solution for respiration detection in complex indoor environments.

中文摘要：在复杂的室内环境中，单模态无线呼吸检测容易受到多径干扰和噪声干扰的影响，导致精度下降。为了解决这一局限性，本文提出了CoSense，一种基于WiFi与毫米波联合感知的非接触式呼吸检测系统。CoSense融合加权信号特征以提升感知精度。首先，本文构建了复杂室内环境下的CSI和FMCW感知模型，并推导出人体呼吸与信号变化之间的映射关系。在此基础上，WiFi分支利用多天线、多子载波CSI构建协方差矩阵，并提取主特征方向，以实现呼吸相关动态分量的自适应合并与噪声抑制；毫米波分支进行二维傅里叶变换，在目标距离门上池化低频多普勒能量以估计呼吸频率。本文进一步设计了一种物理一致的多模态融合策略，基于各模态的置信噪声比构建综合质量因子，采用加权方式得到最终呼吸频率估计值。实验表明，CoSense相比现有方法实现了更高的检测精度，为复杂室内环境下的呼吸检测提供了一种更准确有效的可行方案。

原英文摘要：Reliable detection of slow-moving unmanned aerial vehicles under low signal-to-noise ratio conditions remains a core challenge for next-generation intelligent sensing systems. We propose AirSentinel, a novel deep radar perception network to leverage frame-synchronized real-time kinematic-derived geocoordinates for supervised learning, bridging the gap between image-based and radar-based methods. To address the challenge of weak feature discrimination and energy leakage in radar data, we propose a local search and guard band mechanism that identifies Doppler peaks across coherent processing intervals. We further propose an information weight calibration adapter and a global temporal modeling head to facilitate feature alignment across views and capture long-range temporal dependencies, respectively. To mitigate data scarcity, a object transformation and generation scheme is introduced, significantly boosting data diversity and annotation efficiency. Extensive experiments on the newly curated radarUAV-24K dataset demonstrate that AirSentinel outperforms state-of-the-art methods in both accuracy and real-time performance. This work establishes a robust paradigm for radar-based object detection, with implications for autonomous monitoring and urban airspace security.

## DGHMesh：大规模双雷达毫米波数据集及面向泛化的人体网格重建基准

作者：Rongxiao Guo, Qingchao Chen

出版来源：arXiv

出版时间：2026-04-19

原英文摘要：Millimeter-wave (mmWave) radar has shown great potential for contactless, privacy-preserving, and robust human sensing, yet existing mmWave-based human mesh reconstruction (HMR) studies are still limited by the lack of benchmarks for generalization analysis under configuration shifts and fair comparison of different algorithms. To address the limitation, we present DGHMesh, a large-scale dual-radar mmWave dataset and generalization-focused benchmark for HMR. It contains data from 15 subjects performing 8 actions, with 360,000 synchronized frames collected from FMCW radar, SFCW radar, RGB images, and high-precision 3D HMR annotations. In addition, the dataset provides synchronized raw I/Q data from both radar modalities and accurately calibrated radar spatial positions. The benchmark is designed to evaluate HMR methods under diverse measurement configurations, including human position shifts, human orientation shifts, subarray size variations, and cross-subject settings. Based on DGHMesh, we also propose mmPTM, a query-based multi-radar fusion framework that jointly exploits point clouds and imaging tubes for HMR. Extensive experiments are conducted against representative baselines under different settings. The results demonstrate that mmPTM consistently achieves outstanding accuracy and competitive generalization capability across multiple sub-benchmarks, validating the effectiveness of multi-radar fusion and the practical value of the proposed dataset and benchmark for mmWave-based HMR research. ## 利用毫米波信号和机器学习进行昆虫的无创分类

原英文标题：Harnessing mmWave signals and machine learning for noninvasive taxonomic classification of insects

作者：Linta Antony, Cian White, Nicola Marchetti, Ian Donohue, Jane Catherine Stout, Adam Narbudowicz

出版来源：PNAS Nexus

出版时间：2026-04-28

原英文摘要：Abstract Monitoring insect biodiversity at sufficiently high resolutions in space and time is crucial to underpin robust and responsive management of terrestrial ecosystems. This study presents a novel approach for taxonomic classification of individual pollinating insects using millimeter-wave (mmWave) signal technology and a hierarchical machine learning (ML) framework. Although ML-based species identification has been proposed for image systems, its application to monitoring and classification of insects remains limited, due to high susceptibility to poor image quality and varying light conditions.
