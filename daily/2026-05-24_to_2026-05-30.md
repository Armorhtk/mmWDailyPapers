## 基于贝叶斯估计的旋转轨道SAR建筑物三维成像方法研究 [J]

原英文标题：A Study on 3-D Imaging Method for Buildings Using Rotating Orbital SAR Based on Bayesian Estimation

作者：Jiankun Dong, Yun Lin, Yanping Wang, Wenjie Shen, Wen Jiang, Zechao Bai

出版来源：IEEE Transactions on Instrumentation and Measurement

出版时间：2026-05-20

原英文摘要：Synthetic aperture radar (SAR) is an important remote sensing modality for urban building health monitoring and shows strong potential for 3-D structural reconstruction in building scenes. Leveraging geometric offsets in 2-D projections, SAR 3-D imaging acquires building structural information, addressing 2-D distortion issues. Accordingly, this article proposes a rotating millimeter-wave SAR 3-D imaging mode based on Bayesian estimation, providing a novel preliminary measurement approach for 3-D point-cloud reconstruction of building structures toward building deformation monitoring. To tackle poor imaging quality from sidelobes and speckle noise, a multiangle data acquisition system is established with linear orbital rotation. A layered matching strategy explicitly solves the range–Doppler (R-D) equation, eliminating iterative processes for higher efficiency. In addition, the dense descriptor Daisy is employed for image-pair matching by computing feature descriptors from SAR images. A stereo matching-based mixture distribution model minimizes geometric errors probabilistically to optimize elevation and accelerate 3-D imaging. Experiments using a 77-GHz millimeter-wave radar for multiangle data acquisition and 3-D point-cloud imaging show an imaging accuracy of 0.227 m compared with LiDAR point-cloud heights, verifying the method’s effectiveness. Results obtained from real data acquisition and processing verify the effectiveness of the proposed method and the feasibility of the imaging mode, thereby providing a 3-D structural reconstruction foundation for subsequent high-precision monitoring of building structural deformation.

中文摘要：合成孔径雷达（SAR）是城市建筑健康监测的重要遥感手段，在建筑场景三维结构重建方面展现出巨大潜力。利用二维投影中的几何偏移，SAR三维成像能够获取建筑结构信息，解决二维畸变问题。为此，本文提出一种基于贝叶斯估计的旋转毫米波SAR三维成像模式，为面向建筑形变监测的建筑结构三维点云重建提供一种新颖的初步测量方案。针对旁瓣和散斑噪声导致的成像质量差的问题，建立了基于线性轨道旋转的多角度数据采集系统。一种层间匹配策略显式求解距离-多普勒（R-D）方程，省去迭代过程以提高效率。此外，利用密集描述子Daisy通过计算SAR图像特征描述子进行图像对匹配。基于立体匹配的混合分布模型从概率上最小化几何误差，以优化高程并加速三维成像。采用77 GHz毫米波雷达进行多角度数据采集和三维点云成像的实验表明，与LiDAR点云高度相比，成像精度达到0.227 m，验证了该方法的有效性。实际数据采集与处理的结果验证了所提方法的有效性以及该成像模式的可行性，从而为后续建筑结构形变的高精度监测提供了三维结构重建基础。

## 使用毫米波 FMCW 雷达的长期护理浴室活动监测系统 [J]

原英文标题：Activity Monitoring System for Long-Term Care Bathrooms Using mm-Wave FMCW Radar

作者：Joshua Visser, Hajar Abedi, George Shaker

出版来源：IEEE Sensors Journal

出版时间：2026-05-21

原英文摘要：The growing demand for long-term care (LTC) services, coupled with critical staffing shortages, highlights the need for privacy-preserving solutions to monitor resident safety, particularly in high-risk environments such as bathrooms. This study presents a comprehensive system design and evaluation of an activity monitoring system using a low-cost commercial 60 GHz mm-wave frequency-modulated continuous-wave (FMCW) radar with a single-input multiple-output (SIMO) antenna configuration, designed to recognize both dynamic and static behaviors without compromising resident privacy. The system uses deep learning to leverage spatial radar features (range-elevation and range-Doppler heatmaps), enabling accurate classification of common bathroom activities, including sitting, standing, walking, handwashing, cleaning, and falls. Over 500,000 radar frames were collected across three bathroom layouts with ten participants. The proposed system achieved classification accuracies up to 95% in training environments and maintained over 90% accuracy in new layouts. The system demonstrated consistent fall detection (95% precision), reliable occupancy and multi-person detection, and effective generalization across variable environments through transfer learning. This work addresses a critical gap in LTC monitoring by demonstrating practical, validated deployment of radar-based activity recognition in bathroom environments—one of the highest-risk areas for falls—using commercially available, low-cost hardware. The results demonstrate the system’s potential for real-world deployment, supporting timely intervention and improving safety in vulnerable care settings.

中文摘要：长期护理（LTC）服务需求的不断增长，加上严重的人员短缺，凸显了对隐私保护解决方案的需求，以监测住户安全，特别是在浴室等高危环境中。本研究提出并评估了一种活动监测系统的完整设计方案，该系统使用低成本商用 60 GHz 毫米波调频连续波（FMCW）雷达，采用单输入多输出（SIMO）天线配置，能够在不泄露住户隐私的前提下识别动态和静态行为。该系统利用深度学习，借助空间雷达特征（距离-高度热图和距离-多普勒热图）对常见浴室活动（包括坐着、站立、行走、洗手、清洁和跌倒）进行准确分类。研究在三种浴室布局中，由十位参与者收集了超过 50 万帧雷达数据。所提系统在训练环境中达到高达 95% 的分类准确率，并在新布局中保持超过 90% 的准确率。该系统展示了一致的跌倒检测（95% 精确率）、可靠的占用检测和多人检测，并通过迁移学习在不同环境中有效泛化。本研究通过在实际部署中验证基于雷达的活动识别系统——使用市面上可获取的低成本硬件——在跌倒风险最高的浴室环境中，填补了 LTC 监测领域的关键空白。结果表明该系统具备实际部署潜力，可在脆弱的护理环境中支持及时干预并提高安全性。

## 通过信号调制实现基于FMCW的ISAC中的通信安全与感知隐私 [Preprint]

原英文标题：Communication Security and Sensing Privacy in FMCW-Based ISAC Through Signal Modulation

作者：Murat Temiz, Christos Masouros

出版来源：arXiv

出版时间：2026-05-22

原英文摘要：This study proposes a novel radar-centric signaling design and architecture for secure integrated sensing and communication (ISAC) systems. The proposed framework is designed to provide robust physical layer security for data transmission while simultaneously enhancing sensing privacy. It employs index modulation and phase coding over frequency-modulated continuous-wave radar (FMCW) chirps, where index modulation (IM) provides an outer layer of data security, and we explicitly design the phase coding (PC) to perturb the resulting signal's ambiguity function (AF) to enhance sensing privacy. This design reduces the risk of unauthorized surveillance by rendering target velocity estimation practically infeasible for unauthorized passive sensing hardware (i.e., a sensing eavesdropper, S-Eve) and significantly impairing its range estimation capabilities. Furthermore, this study also presents the transmitter and receiver architectures required for effective modulation and demodulation of the proposed ISAC signaling and for performing sensing at the legitimate sensing hardware. Simulation results show that the proposed approach achieves high data throughput while enhancing communication security and sensing privacy.

中文摘要：本研究提出了一种新颖的以雷达为中心的信号设计与架构，用于安全集成感知与通信（ISAC）系统。该框架旨在为数据传输提供鲁棒的物理层安全性，同时增强感知隐私。它在调频连续波雷达（FMCW）chirp上采用索引调制和相位编码，其中索引调制（IM）提供了外层数据安全，而我们显式设计相位编码（PC）来扰动所得信号的模糊函数（AF），以增强感知隐私。该设计通过使目标速度估计对非授权被动感知硬件（即感知窃听者，S-Eve）实际不可行，并显著削弱其距离估计能力，从而降低了未授权监视的风险。此外，本研究还提出了有效调制和解调所提出的ISAC信号以及合法感知硬件执行感知所需的发射机和接收机架构。仿真结果表明，所提方法在提高通信安全性和感知隐私的同时实现了高数据吞吐量。

## 基于NLOS感知的移动机器人转角导航算法设计 [M]

原英文标题：Corner Navigation Algorithm Design with NLOS Sensing for Mobile Robots

作者：Beichen Yu, Haiming Jin, Guiyun Fan

出版来源：Frontiers of Networking Technologies

出版时间：2026-05-01

原英文摘要：In order to achieve efficient and reliable destination reaching for indoor navigation, mobile robots must accurately perceive their surroundings utilizing multi-modal sensors, dynamically avoid various obstacles, and plan optimal trajectories. In practice, there usually exist obstacles (e.g., walls, cabinets) blocking the sight of mobile robots when they reach the corner or the intersections. Sensors equipped on mobile robots like LiDAR and depth cameras often fail to detect occluded pedestrians and obstacles, potentially leading to suboptimal path planning. Therefore, enabling around-corner sensing can help prevent potential collision by triggering better path planning. For various sensors equipped on mobile robots, mmWave radar provides the opportunity to perceive conditions of around-corner obstacles via multi-bounce signal reflection through wall. In this paper, we achieve the non-line-of-sight (NLOS) sensing by utilizing mmWave Radar with LiDAR assistance. Furthermore, we propose an obstacle-aware corner navigation algorithm based on reinforcement learning (RL) methods, which leverages the NLOS sensing results for corner navigation. Besides, in order to address the challenge that the position and orientation of the mmWave radar significantly influence the performance of NLOS sensing, we design a specialized RL architecture where mmWave radar’s instant observations are embedded into the optimization objective for path planning. Experiments show that our proposed method enables more accurate NLOS sensing for mobile robot corner navigation, and significantly outperforms the baseline method with respect to the navigation success rate.

中文摘要：为了实现室内导航中高效可靠的到达目标，移动机器人必须利用多模态传感器准确感知周围环境，动态规避各种障碍物，并规划最优轨迹。实际上，当移动机器人到达转角或交叉口时，通常存在障碍物（如墙壁、柜子）遮挡其视线。移动机器人上配备的传感器如LiDAR和深度摄像头往往无法检测到被遮挡的行人和障碍物，可能导致次优路径规划。因此，实现转角感知有助于通过触发更好的路径规划来预防潜在碰撞。对于移动机器人上配备的各类传感器，毫米波雷达通过墙壁的多径信号反射提供了感知转角后障碍物状况的机会。本文通过结合LiDAR辅助的毫米波雷达实现了非视距（NLOS）感知。此外，我们提出了一种基于强化学习（RL）方法的障碍物感知转角导航算法，该算法利用NLOS感知结果进行转角导航。为了应对毫米波雷达的位置和朝向显著影响NLOS感知性能的挑战，我们设计了一种专门的RL架构，其中毫米波雷达的即时观测被嵌入到路径规划的优化目标中。实验表明，我们提出的方法能够实现更精确的移动机器人转角NLOS感知，并在导航成功率上显著优于基线方法。

## 跨域雷达手势生成：基于带有迁移引导和物理先验的扩散模型 [J]

作者：Fubiao Yang, Yuanyuan Wang, Yanhua Yin, Xiaoxu Han, Yi Zhang

出版来源：IEEE Sensors Journal

出版时间：2026-05-25

原英文摘要：Radar-based gesture recognition has shown great potential in human-computer interaction due to its strong privacy-preserving capabilities. However, practical deployment is severely constrained by factors such as device installation limitations, environmental variability, and high data acquisition costs. These challenges lead to sample scarcity and cross-domain distribution shifts, which significantly degrade both generation and recognition performance. Existing generative methods suffer from mode collapse under extreme data scarcity and produce samples lacking kinematic physical constraints, compromising structural plausibility. To address these challenges, this paper proposes Transfer-Guided Diffusion Process with Physical Prior (TGDP-P), a diffusion-based radar gesture generation method that synergistically integrates transfer guidance with physical priors. During the reverse denoising process of the diffusion model, TGDP-P incorporates a transfer-guidance mechanism and physical prior constraints. Specifically, a density-ratio correction term is introduced to efficiently bridge the distribution gap between source and target domains, while time-frequency ridge regularization enforces kinematic consistency in the generated samples. Ablation studies confirm that the physical prior is critical to generation quality, and the synergy between distribution guidance and physical constraints enables TGDP-P to generate samples with both high fidelity and strong physical plausibility. Experiments on a simulated dataset and the public UWB-Gestures benchmark demonstrate that the proposed method maintains superior performance under extreme data scarcity. With 100 samples from the target domain, TGDP-P achieves an F1-score of 0.7970 and improves classification accuracy by 2.60 percentage points compared to the baseline. Furthermore, quantitative evaluation across four representative cross-domain scenarios validates the strong generalization capability of TGDP-P, offering a high-fidelity and robust data generation solution for radar-based gesture recognition under resource-constrained conditions.

## 数字孪生用于FMCW雷达检测中的紧凑混合量子经典学习 [Preprint]

原英文标题：Digital twins for compact hybrid quantum classical learning in FMCW radar detection

作者：Sebastian Ratto Valderrama, Ahmed N. Sayed, Arien Sligar, Jose R. Rosas-Bustos, Omar M. Ramahi, George Shaker

出版来源：arXiv

出版时间：2026-05-22

原英文摘要：Frequency-modulated continuous-wave radar sensing often relies on labeled measurements that are costly, restricted, or difficult to collect at scale. This work evaluates physics-informed digital twins as controlled testbeds for early-stage quantum-classical radar learning. Two synthetic radar benchmarks are considered: unmanned aerial vehicle classification from range-Doppler maps and human fall detection from Doppler-time spectrograms. For both tasks, inputs are standardized, reduced using principal component analysis, and classified using either a radial basis function support vector classifier or a quantum support vector classifier. All quantum-kernel results are obtained using noiseless classical simulation; no quantum hardware is used, and no quantum-advantage claim is made. Across five random seeds, the quantum support vector classifier improves the UAV benchmark from four principal components onward, reaching an accuracy of 0.941 +/- 0.012 at eight components, compared with 0.880 +/- 0.029 for the classical baseline. On the fall-detection benchmark, both classifiers perform similarly, with a small quantum-kernel improvement at higher feature dimensions.
