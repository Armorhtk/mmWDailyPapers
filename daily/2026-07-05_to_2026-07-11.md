## 基于RDTM和轻量SS-EMA网络的不平衡样本毫米波雷达手势识别方法 [J]

原英文标题：A Method Based on RDTM and Lightweight SS-EMA Network for Imbalanced Sample Gesture Recognition Using mmWave Radar

作者：Huixi Wei, Jiaxin Zheng, Peihao Yuan, Demin Kong, ShiSheng Guo, Guolong Cui, Pengyun Chen, Mingliang Xu

出版来源：IEEE Internet of Things Journal

出版时间：2026-07-06

原英文摘要：Millimeter-wave radar-based hand gesture recognition (HGR) is a promising non-contact human-machine interaction technology in the Industrial Internet of Things (IoT) field. Most gesture recognition models assume balanced data distributions, but in practice, datasets are often imbalanced, with common gestures dominating and rare ones being under-represented. This imbalance degrades performance on minority classes, while the high computational complexity of existing models limits their deployment on resource-constrained edge devices. We propose a lightweight architecture to address these issues. First, three-dimensional radar data is obtained through channel non-coherent integration and frame temporal accumulation, which is then converted into two-dimensional Range-Doppler-Time Maps (RDTMs). This conversion significantly reduces data dimensionality and computational complexity, while effectively enhancing the signal-to-noise ratio (SNR) and preserving both the range and velocity characteristics of gestures. Second, a lightweight dual-residual attention architecture is introduced to achieve efficient multi-scale feature fusion with minimal computational overhead. Furthermore, the architecture employs an exponential moving average (EMA) Loss, which dynamically adjusts training weights across different classes to strengthen feature learning for minority gestures. Extensive experiments were conducted on a self-constructed six-class gesture dataset with five different imbalance ratios (IRs). The proposed method achieves 97.33% accuracy with only 3.2M parameters at an imbalance ratio of 24, along with 93.22% Macro-F1 and 93.03% G-mean. These results confirm both the efficiency and robustness of the hand gesture recognition method.

原英文摘要：We first present a wideband millimeter-wave sparse array of novel wideband laminated resonant antennas (LRAs) tailored for high-resolution radars applications. 作者：Boyi Zhang, Jie Wang, Xinjing Qin, Xiaokai Liu, Qinghua Gao, Miao Pan, Yuguang Fang

出版来源：IEEE Transactions on Mobile Computing

出版时间：2026-07-01

原英文摘要：Deep learning-based wireless sensing can effectively learn implicit patterns involved in wireless signals influenced by a target, enabling various sensing tasks. However, collecting and labeling wireless samples are time-consuming and labor-intensive, resulting in a limited number of training samples. This prevents sensing networks from learning implicit patterns, leading to degraded sensing performance. To achieve high-performance wireless sensing with limited samples, we propose a multi-domain diffusion (MD-Diffusion) model to generate abundant virtual samples and improve sensing performance. The proposed MD-Diffusion model utilizes the diversity of wireless sample features across multiple domains, including time, frequency, wavelet, and intrinsic mode domains, to enhance the quality of virtual samples. Specifically, we design a multi-domain diffusion based forward process to gradually perturb the multi-domain representations of wireless samples across different domains, until they become noise, enabling the reverse process to learn diverse sample features at various noise levels. We also design a multi-domain U-Net based reverse process to utilize the multi-domain sample features for reconstructing sample distributions, thus enhancing the sample generation capability of the MD-Diffusion model. By inputting noise into the MD-Diffusion model, virtual samples will be generated. Experimental results demonstrate that MD-Diffusion model outperforms mainstream generative models in generating FMCW and WiFi samples.

## 面向车载雷达目标检测的自适应图构造学习 [J]

原英文标题：Adaptive Graph Construction Learning for Automotive Radar Object Detection

作者：Xiuping Li, Xiyan Sun, Jingjing Li, Yuanfa Ji, Mou Ma

出版来源：IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Transportation Systems

出版时间：2026-07-03

原英文摘要：Millimeter-wave automotive radar is essential for autonomous driving due to its robustness under adverse weather conditions. However, radar point clouds are inherently sparse, noisy, and of low resolution, posing significant challenges for object detection. Graph neural networks (GNNs) are a natural fit for radar point clouds, yet conventional approaches rely on static, heuristic graph constructions such as fixed $k$ -nearest neighbors ( $k$ -NN) that ignore radar-specific multi-attribute cues including Doppler velocity and radar cross-section (RCS). This paper presents Adaptive Graph Construction Learning (AGCL), a framework in which a positive semidefinite (PSD) Mahalanobis metric is learned offline to reweight edges on a sparse candidate graph, yielding feature-aware connectivity. The metric learning is formulated by minimizing a graph Laplacian regularizer and is performed as a one-time preprocessing step for each point-cloud graph; the resulting edge weights are cached and reused during detector training and inference, incurring low online overhead. A block-wise optimization strategy enables scalability to large graphs. Two instantiations are considered: a full PSD metric (AGCL-L) evaluated on RadarScenes and a constrained diagonal variant (AGCL-D) for lightweight plug-in integration on View-of-Delft. The learned feature graph is integrated into downstream detectors either as edge weights for GNN message passing (AGCL-Edge) or as neighborhood-statistics feature augmentation (AGCL-Stats), requiring only interface-level changes rather than backbone redesign. On RadarScenes, AGCL improves over a strong RadarGNN baseline; additional gains are also validated on View-of-Delft using modern detector families including RadarPillars and LEROjD/DSVT. Ablation studies confirm the benefit of incorporating Doppler and RCS cues.

中文摘要：毫米波车载雷达因在恶劣天气条件下的鲁棒性而成为自动驾驶的关键传感器。然而，雷达点云本质上稀疏、含噪且分辨率低，给目标检测带来巨大挑战。图神经网络（GNN）天然适用于雷达点云，但传统方法依赖静态启发式图构造（如固定k近邻），忽略了雷达特有的多属性线索（如多普勒速度和雷达截面积RCS）。本文提出自适应图构造学习（AGCL）框架，离线学习正半定（PSD）马氏度量来重加权稀疏候选图上的边，生成特征感知的连接关系。该度量学习通过最小化图拉普拉斯正则项来定义，并作为每个点云图的一次性预处理步骤执行；得到的边权重被缓存并在检测器训练和推理期间复用，在线开销极低。块级优化策略使方法可扩展到大规模图。考虑两种实例化：在RadarScenes上评估的全PSD度量（AGCL-L）和在View-of-Delft上轻量即插即用的受限对角变体（AGCL-D）。学习到的特征图可作为GNN消息传递的边权重（AGCL-Edge）或邻域统计特征增强（AGCL-Stats）集成到下游检测器中，仅需接口级改动而非骨干网络重设计。在RadarScenes上，AGCL比强大的RadarGNN基线有提升；在View-of-Delft上使用RadarPillars和LEROjD/DSVT等现代检测器系列也验证了额外增益。消融研究证实了纳入多普勒和RCS线索的收益。

原英文摘要：Noncontact micro–displacement measurement in dynamic environments is essential for healthcare and infrastructure monitoring. This study proposes a signal–processing architecture that addresses this structural limitation by sequentially integrating adaptive clutter suppression (with adaptive moving target indication, MTI) and continuous–space phase optimization (with range–phase non–harmonic analysis, NHA). 原英文摘要：Ubiquitous Wi-Fi signals capture rich spatiotemporal features of human gestures. To address this limitation, we propose D-Sense, a multi-task sensing system built on Channel State Information (CSI).
