## 400-GHz 并发收发像素：用于短距离 FMCW 雷达成像

原英文标题：400-GHz Concurrent Transceiver Pixels for Short Range FMCW Radar Imaging

作者：Goutham Murugesan, Muhammad Awais, Sarfraz Shariff, Frank Zhang, Wooyeol Choi, Kenneth K. O.

出版来源：IEEE Transactions on Microwave Theory and Techniques

出版时间：2026-04-22

原英文摘要：A 400-GHz concurrent transceiver imaging pixel with co-optimized output power, locking range (LR), and receiver noise performance is reported. The pixel integrates TX, RX, injection locking circuitry, and patch antenna within a ( $\lambda $ /2) 2 area, which makes the pixel more easily scalable to form focal plane arrays. The transceiver pixel is fabricated in the GF 22-nm FDSOI CMOS process with a chip area of $740\times 520$ $\mu $ m and achieves a measured peak effective isotropic radiated power (EIRP) of −14.8 dBm, a measured minimum DSB NF of 33dB, and an LR of 8.5 GHz while consuming only 63.2 mW of DC power at 0.8-V V DD . The operation of the pixel with an FMCW injection signal is demonstrated over 8.4 GHz at a ramp rate of 174 MHz/ $\mu $ s. Using a module assembled with a PCB, including a pixel IC and a 6-cm-diameter Cassegrain reflector, radar imaging of a pair of corner reflectors at 1.85 and 2 m away with a cardboard cover is demonstrated. These design and characterization efforts were used to establish a design framework/flow for concurrent transceiver pixels.

## FMCW雷达生命体征信号降噪算法

原英文标题：A noise reduction algorithm for FMCW radar vital signs signals

作者：Min Xie, Xiaowen Du, Yiming Chen, Xuefeng Li, Meng Zhao, Jing Ji

出版来源：Measurement

出版时间：2026-04-23

原英文摘要：Recent years have witnessed remarkable advancements in smart healthcare, particularly in non-contact respiration and heartbeat detection technologies utilizing Frequency Modulated Continuous Wave (FMCW) radar, which has garnered significant research attention. However, during vital signs detection using FMCW radar, multiple interference factors, such as hardware noise and environmental noise, compromise the accuracy of respiratory and heartbeat detection. To address this issue, this paper proposes a novel denoising method integrating Improved Black Kite Optimization Algorithm (IBKA) optimized Variational Mode Decomposition (VMD) with Wavelet Thresholding (WT), and validates its effectiveness using both simulated and real signals. On simulated signals with varying noise levels, the proposed denoising algorithm achieves a higher Signal-to-Noise Ratio (SNR) compared with the two reference denoising algorithms: Empirical Mode Decomposition with Wavelet Thresholding (EMD-WT) and Complete Ensemble Empirical Mode Decomposition with Adaptive Noise with Wavelet Thresholding (CEEMDAN-WT). Experimental results on real signals demonstrate that the proposed algorithm achieves significant noise rejection ratio enhancements of 58.12% and 43.16% respectively over the two reference methods. This work has important practical significance for the accurate detection of respiration and heartbeat in subsequent analyses.

中文摘要：近年来，智能医疗领域取得了显著进展，特别是利用调频连续波（FMCW）雷达的非接触式呼吸和心跳检测技术，已引起广泛研究关注。然而，使用FMCW雷达进行生命体征检测时，硬件噪声和环境噪声等多重干扰因素会降低呼吸和心跳检测的准确性。针对这一问题，本文提出了一种结合改进黑鸢优化算法（IBKA）优化的变分模态分解（VMD）与小波阈值（WT）的新型去噪方法，并通过模拟信号和真实信号验证了其有效性。在不同噪声水平的模拟信号上，与两种参考去噪算法（经验模态分解结合小波阈值 EMD-WT，以及自适应噪声完全集合经验模态分解结合小波阈值 CEEMDAN-WT）相比，所提去噪算法获得了更高的信噪比（SNR）。真实信号实验结果表明，与两种参考方法相比，所提算法的噪声抑制比分别提升了58.12%和43.16%。该工作对后续分析中呼吸和心跳的准确检测具有重要的实际意义。

## 实时嵌入式毫米波雷达系统用于非接触式震颤评估

作者：Haoxuan Li, Tiantao Jiang, Rui Fan, Liang Chen, Anne Bernassau, Kevin Chetty, Wenda Li

出版来源：UCL Discovery

出版时间：2026-04-20

原英文摘要：Tremor is a common symptom of Parkinson's disease (PD) and other movement disorders, and accurate monitoring is important for disease management. This paper presents a real-time, contactless tremor quantification system using a 60 GHz frequency-modulated continuous-wave (FMCW) millimeter-wave (mmWave) radar integrated with Raspberry Pi platforms. To generate clinically relevant tremor-like motion under controlled yet realistic human conditions, a medical-grade transcutaneous electrical nerve stimulation (TENS) device applies pulsed currents to forearm muscles, producing repeatable oscillations at prescribed stimulation frequencies with tremor-like amplitudes. The full embedded pipeline includes static clutter suppression, range-Doppler processing, range-gated slow-time extraction, tremor-band filtering, spectral enhancement, and automatic peak detection. Multi-channel spectral fusion improves robustness to background motion and supports directional sensitivity assessment. The system outputs dominant frequency, normalised peak strength, and spectral energy concentration. Experiments on healthy volunteers wearing TENS show stable dominant-frequency tracking across practical stand-off distances and incidence angles up to +/-60 degrees. The system also supports ROI (Range of Interested) separation, including identical-frequency cases via range gating. On embedded hardware, the end-to-end processing latency per frame is below 50 ms with power consumption below 5 W, enabling continuous, non-invasive monitoring in clinical and home settings.

## 使用不可听声学FMCW的活动识别

出版时间：2026-05-08

原英文摘要：Inaudible acoustic signals can be used to recognize human activities. Existing approaches exploit Doppler effect on fixed frequency acoustic signals to infer activities. However, they suffer from incapability of detecting static targets, discriminating similar activities, and generalizing to unseen domains. In this paper, we leverage Frequency-Modulated Continuous Wave (FMCW) of inaudible acoustic signals to achieve accurate and generalizable activity recognition as well as static target detection. We construct two novel signal features from raw FMCW chirps: FMCW Time-Distance Profile (FTDP) and Normalized Cross-Correlation Envelope (NCCE), which are fused to enable accurate activity recognition. To alleviate data scarcity, we propose a text-guided diffusion-based generator to synthesize acoustic activity data, conforming to acoustic propagation characteristics and activity semantic features. To enhance generalization, we propose Adaptive Gradient Reversal Layer (AGRL) and integrate Gradient Normalization (GradNorm) to optimize the multi-task training. Extensive experiments demonstrate the effectiveness.

原英文摘要：Near-field millimeter-wave (mmWave) imaging is widely deployed in safety-critical applications such as airport passenger screening, yet its own security remains largely unexplored. This paper presents a systematic study of the adversarial robustness of mmWave imaging algorithms under waveform-domain physical attacks that directly manipulate the image reconstruction process. We propose a practical white-box adversarial model and develop a differential imaging attack framework that leverages the differentiable imaging pipeline to optimize attack waveforms. We also construct a real measured dataset of clean and attack waveforms using a mmWave imaging testbed. ## 基于深度生成压缩感知的毫米波系统信道估计

原英文标题：Channel Estimation for mmWave Systems via Deep Generative Compressed Sensing

作者：Beomsoo Ko, Gyoseung Lee, Yongjun Kim, Junil Choi

出版来源：IEEE Transactions on Wireless Communications

出版时间：2026-04-23

原英文摘要：Millimeter wave (mmWave) multiple-input multiple-output (MIMO) communication systems require accurate channel estimation for effective beamforming. Conventional compressed sensing (CS) algorithms utilize the inherent sparsity information of channels but suffer from basis mismatch problems for on-grid methods and high computational complexity for off-grid methods. Recently, generative model-based CS frameworks have shown promising results by directly learning the distribution of channel realizations, eliminating the need for prior sparsity information. However, these approaches rely on non-convex optimization in latent space that may converge to suboptimal solutions and exhibit generalization problems across diverse channel environments. Moreover, existing generative approaches have not adequately addressed the quantization effects of practical finite-resolution analog-to-digital converters (ADCs). To address these limitations, we propose a novel generative CS (GCS) algorithm using a conditional variational autoencoder (CVAE) that learns fundamental path components of mmWave MIMO channels rather than complete channel realizations while considering finite-resolution quantization. The CVAE is trained on single-path measurements with spatial frequency conditioning variables, enabling generalization across diverse channel environments without requiring environment-specific training data. Our algorithm directly encodes quantized received pilot signals and angular conditioning variables into latent representations, which are then fed into a decoder to output reconstructions conditioned on the angular parameters, with the decoder trained to be robust to perturbations from additive and quantization noise. The algorithm sequentially identifies path components by searching through spatial frequency codebooks with different conditioning values and applying gradient-based refinement for off-grid accuracy. Numerical results demonstrate that the proposed GCS algorithm outperforms conventional CS algorithms across various scenarios.

中文摘要：毫米波（mmWave）多输入多输出（MIMO）通信系统需要精确的信道估计以实现有效的波束赋形。传统的压缩感知（CS）算法利用信道的固有稀疏信息，但基于网格的方法存在基失配问题，而离网格方法则计算复杂度高。最近，基于生成模型的CS框架通过学习信道实现分布的直方图展示了有前景的结果，无需先验稀疏信息。然而，这些方法依赖于隐空间中的非凸优化，可能收敛到次优解，并在不同信道环境下出现泛化问题。此外，现有生成方法未能充分处理实际有限分辨率模数转换器（ADC）的量化效应。为解决这些问题，我们提出了一种新的生成压缩感知（GCS）算法，使用条件变分自编码器（CVAE）学习mmWave MIMO信道的基本路径分量，而非完整的信道实现，同时考虑有限分辨率量化。CVAE在具有空间频率条件变量的单路径测量上进行训练，从而能够跨不同信道环境泛化，无需特定环境的训练数据。我们的算法直接将量化后的接收导频信号和角度条件变量编码为隐表示，然后输入解码器输出以角度参数为条件的重建结果，且解码器经过训练对加性噪声和量化噪声的扰动具有鲁棒性。该算法通过搜索具有不同条件值的空间频率码本并应用基于梯度的修正来实现离网格精度，从而顺序识别路径分量。数值结果表明，所提出的GCS算法在各种场景下均优于传统CS算法。
