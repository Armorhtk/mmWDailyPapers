## 低复杂度高效空间重采样技术：用于4D高分辨率MIMO FMCW雷达系统的距离-角度去耦合

原英文标题：A Low-Complexity and Efficient Spatial Resampling Technique for Range–Angle Decoupling With 4-D High-Resolution MIMO FMCW Radar System

作者：Jiayu Zhang, Yuchen Li, Zhiwei Zhang, Changzhan Gu, Junfa Mao

出版来源：IEEE Transactions on Microwave Theory and Techniques

出版时间：2026-03-30

原英文摘要：Multiple-input multiple-output (MIMO) frequency-modulated continuous wave (FMCW) radar systems have been widely adopted in advanced sensing applications due to affording superior resolution and accuracy. However, when both a wide modulation bandwidth and a large aperture are incorporated, which are recognized as essential features of the high-resolution radar systems, performance degradation is often introduced due to the range-angle coupling (RAC) effect. This phenomenon impairs resolution in both range and angular domains, with especially severe degradation occurring at off-boresight angles. Although various compensation techniques have been proposed, most of them involve substantial complexity and have limited practical applicability. To address these challenges, a low-complexity and efficient spatial resampling technique (SRT) is proposed, in which the discrete frequency components of the intermediate frequency (IF) signal are utilized to perform resampling in the virtual elements. For each frequency bin, the resampling process is conducted based on the corresponding wavelength, instead of the physical wavelength. By aligning the spatial sampling grid with the wavelength of each frequency across the entire modulation bandwidth, the inconsistency in phase differences across virtual array elements is effectively eliminated. Subsequent 2-D angle-range fast Fourier transforms (FFTs) are applied, ensuring each target to be concentrated within its correct cell and effectively mitigating resolution degradation. In contrast to the conventional techniques that typically introduce higher orders of complexity, the proposed technique computational burden is constituted almost entirely by highly efficient FFT operations, ensuring that an exceptionally low complexity is maintained, with an improvement of 100 times over existing methods. Experiments have been conducted to validate the proposed technique. The technique provides a practical solution for high-resolution, low-cost MIMO FMCW radar by addressing a fundamental wave-propagation issue inherent to wideband large-aperture radar systems.

中文摘要：多输入多输出（MIMO）调频连续波（FMCW）雷达系统由于提供卓越的分辨率和精度，已被广泛应用于先进感知应用。然而，当同时采用宽调制带宽和大孔径（被认为是高分辨率雷达系统的基本特征）时，由于距离-角度耦合（RAC）效应，往往会导致性能下降。该现象会损害距离和角度域的分辨率，尤其在偏离瞄准轴的角度处退化尤为严重。尽管已提出各种补偿技术，但大多数复杂度较高且实际适用性有限。为应对这些挑战，提出了一种低复杂度且高效的空间重采样技术（SRT），其中利用中频（IF）信号的离散频率分量在虚拟阵元上进行重采样。对于每个频率单元，重采样过程基于对应的波长而非物理波长进行。通过将空间采样网格与整个调制带宽内每个频率的波长对齐，有效消除了虚拟阵列单元之间的相位差不一致性。随后应用二维角度-距离快速傅里叶变换（FFT），确保每个目标被集中到正确的单元内，从而有效缓解分辨率退化。与通常引入更高复杂度的传统技术相比，所提技术的计算负担几乎全部由高效的FFT操作构成，确保了极低的复杂度，相较于现有方法提升了100倍。通过实验验证了所提技术。该技术通过解决宽带大孔径雷达系统固有的基本波传播问题，为高分辨率、低成本的MIMO FMCW雷达提供了一种实用解决方案。

## ClaRO：基于聚类的雷达里程计方法

原英文标题：ClaRO: A Cluster-Based Method for Radar Odometry

作者：Eike Furuno, Pranav Megarajan, Christian Kowalski, Tim C. Stratmann, Max Pfingsthorn, Andreas Hein

出版来源：IEEE Robotics and Automation Letters

出版时间：2026-04-20

原英文摘要：We present ClaRO, an unsupervised, cluster-based odometry pipeline for 4D imaging radar to improve robustness and reduce long-term drift for radar-only odometry. Our core idea is to utilize density-based clustering to improve ego-motion estimation using per-cluster Doppler-residuals and find a local best result to seed a robust least squares in addition to finding static points for pose estimation. The resulting global inlier mask is used to refine the pose via a weighted iterative closest point (ICP) approach that fuses Doppler and radar cross-section (RCS) cues while down-weighting the radar's weak elevation axis. The method is fully unsupervised and sensor-agnostic. We compare our method on multiple public datasets (View-of-Delft, HeRCULES, and NTU4DRadLM) and show that our approach provides results that match or exceed recent radar-only odometry baselines (Radar4Motion, EFEAR-4D) and pose graph based 4DRadarSLAM. Our cluster-wise approach significantly reduces long-term drift, achieving a 72.0% improvement in mean absolute trajectory error (ATE) and a 73.2% improvement in absolute rotation error (ARE) compared to state-of-the-art radar odometry methods on the HeRCULES dataset. While remaining competitive in relative pose estimation, our method improves global trajectory consistency and demonstrates robustness across different radar sensors and environments on the VoD and NTU4DRadLM benchmarks.

中文摘要：我们提出 ClaRO，一种无监督的、基于聚类的 4D 成像雷达里程计流水线，旨在提升纯雷达里程计的鲁棒性并减少长期漂移。核心思路是利用基于密度的聚类，通过每聚类多普勒残差改进自运动估计，并找到局部最优结果来初始化鲁棒最小二乘，同时为位姿估计提取静态点。最终的内点掩码用于通过加权迭代最近点（ICP）方法精化位姿，该方法融合多普勒和雷达散射截面（RCS）线索，同时对雷达较弱的仰角轴进行降权。该方法完全无监督且与传感器无关。我们在多个公开数据集（View-of-Delft、HeRCULES 和 NTU4DRadLM）上进行了比较，结果表明我们的方法达到或超过了近期纯雷达里程计基线（Radar4Motion、EFEAR-4D）和基于位姿图的 4DRadarSLAM 的性能。我们的逐聚类方法显著减少了长期漂移，在 HeRCULES 数据集上相比最先进的雷达里程计方法，平均绝对轨迹误差（ATE）改进了 72.0%，绝对旋转误差（ARE）改进了 73.2%。在相对位姿估计方面保持竞争力的同时，我们的方法提升了全局轨迹一致性，并在 VoD 和 NTU4DRadLM 基准上展示了在不同雷达传感器和环境中的鲁棒性。

原英文标题：Vegetation Height Estimation Using a 77-80 GHz Millimeter-Wave Radar

作者：Daniel Espinal-de-la-Cruz, Mark Clemente-Arenas, Jinmi Lezama-Calvo, Sleiter Ramos-Sanchez

出版来源：2026 IEEE 2nd Latin American Conference on Antennas and Propagation (LACAP)

原英文摘要：This paper investigates a low-cost $77-80 \text{GHz}$ FMCW millimeter-wave radar workflow for short-range forest canopy-height estimation. Radar echoes collected along linear trajectories in front of real trees are processed with a standard FMCW chain (range FFT, log-magnitude, smoothing, and CFAR) to generate a point cloud and a simple ground profile.
