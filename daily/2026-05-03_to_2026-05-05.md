## 多模态数据融合的毫米波通信波束追踪方法

原英文标题：A Beam Tracking Approach for mmWave Communication with Multi-Modal Data Fusion

作者：Xin Xie, Feiyue Liao, Heng Wang

出版来源：IEEE Transactions on Vehicular Technology

出版时间：2026-04-29

原英文摘要：Millimeter wave (mmWave) communication is a key technology for the future communication. Using multi-modal data for beam tracking can effectively ensure the efficiency of mmWave communication. However, existing methods often suffer challenges such as inconsistent feature representation and misaligned data between modalities, which leads to information loss or interference during the fusion process, ultimately affecting the accuracy of beam tracking and the overall performance of the mmWave communication system. To solve the above problems, we propose a multi-modal data fusion beam tracking method based on contrastive learning, which can enhance the feature alignment capability between different modal data and improve the effect of data fusion. The test results show that the proposed scheme can achieve higher beam tracking accuracy compared to existing state-of-the-art algorithms.

中文摘要：毫米波（mmWave）通信是未来通信的关键技术。利用多模态数据进行波束追踪可以有效保障毫米波通信的效率。然而，现有方法常面临特征表示不一致、模态间数据未对齐等挑战，导致融合过程中信息丢失或干扰，最终影响波束追踪精度及毫米波通信系统整体性能。为解决上述问题，我们提出了一种基于对比学习的多模态数据融合波束追踪方法，能够增强不同模态数据间的特征对齐能力，提升数据融合效果。测试结果表明，与现有最优算法相比，所提方案能实现更高的波束追踪精度。

## 基于FMCW雷达的双通道CNN-CA手势识别模型

原英文标题：A dual-channel CNN-CA model for gesture recognition using FMCW radar

作者：Weihao Wang, Chen Ding

出版来源：Physical Communication

出版时间：2026-04-29

原英文摘要：This paper proposes a dual-channel CNN-CA model based on frequency-modulated continuous wave (FMCW) radar for gesture recognition. The model fuses distance information and micro-Doppler features of gestures, incorporating a coordinate attention (CA) mechanism to enhance gesture recognition accuracy. First, the raw FMCW echo signal is pre-processed by removing the DC component, reorganizing the data, and applying a 2D-FFT transformation to extract the time-varying distance and micro-Doppler information of the gestures. The coherent accumulation method is then employed to improve the signal-to-noise ratio. Subsequently, the extracted distance and micro-Doppler features are fed into a dual-channel model. After passing through the convolutional neural network (CNN), feature fusion is performed, followed by the integration of the coordinate attention mechanism to further improve the model's performance. The proposed dual-channel CNN-CA model is compared with several traditional models, including VGG-16, DC-CNN, CNN-LSTM, CNN-SE and CNN-Transformer. Experimental results demonstrate that the recognition accuracy of the proposed model surpasses that of the traditional models by 5.65%, 4.28%, 3.18%, 3.72% and 1.98%, respectively, indicating a significant improvement in gesture recognition performance. This model shows considerable potential for application in the field of gesture recognition.

中文摘要：本文提出了一种基于调频连续波（FMCW）雷达的双通道CNN-CA模型用于手势识别。该模型融合手势的距离信息与微多普勒特征，并引入坐标注意力（CA）机制以提升手势识别精度。首先，对原始FMCW回波信号进行预处理，包括去除直流分量、数据重排以及二维FFT变换，以提取手势的时变距离和微多普勒信息。然后采用相干积累方法提高信噪比。随后，将提取的距离和微多普勒特征输入双通道模型。经过卷积神经网络（CNN）后，进行特征融合，并进一步集成坐标注意力机制以提升模型性能。所提出的双通道CNN-CA模型与多种传统模型（包括VGG-16、DC-CNN、CNN-LSTM、CNN-SE和CNN-Transformer）进行了对比。实验结果表明，该模型的识别精度分别比传统模型高出5.65%、4.28%、3.18%、3.72%和1.98%，显著提升了手势识别性能。该模型在手势识别领域具有重要的应用潜力。

## ADEPT：用于复合材料的毫米波SFCW层析成像框架

原英文标题：A Millimeter-Wave SFCW Tomography Framework With ADEPT for Composite Materials

作者：Huanyu Sun, Chen Li, Lingyu Liu, Jiaojie Chen, Haoyu Wang, Xiuwei Yang, Zhongmin Wang

出版来源：IEEE Transactions on Microwave Theory and Techniques

出版时间：2026-04-28

原英文摘要：Millimeter-wave (mmW) stepped-frequency continuous-wave (SFCW) imaging presents a promising approach for nondestructive evaluation (NDE), yet its full potential remains constrained by unresolved physical and signal-processing challenges. This work introduces a unified mmW SFCW tomography framework that advances the entire signal chain from theoretical modeling to hardware design and reconstruction algorithms. A physics-based analytical model is developed to describe SFCW propagation and multi-interface echo formation in composite materials. Guided by this model, a modular platform is implemented by integrating a vector network analyzer (VNA), an mmW extension module, and a “4f” quasioptical focusing system. Furthermore, an adaptive defect enhancement with phase-assisted tomography (ADEPT) algorithm is proposed, incorporating phase-based geometric referencing and reference-based enhancement to extract subtle defect signatures and support 3-D reconstruction. Experiments in the 75–110 GHz range visualize typical defects in foam-core sandwich composites, achieving 1 mm lateral and 0.5 mm axial sensitivity, a penetration depth beyond 20 mm, and a dynamic range of 45 dB. The proposed framework establishes a physics-consistent and scalable foundation for mmW SFCW imaging and its application to composite materials inspection.

中文摘要：毫米波（mmW）步进频率连续波（SFCW）成像为非破坏性评估（NDE）提供了一种有前景的方法，但其全部潜力仍受到未解决的物理和信号处理挑战的限制。本文提出了一种统一的毫米波SFCW层析成像框架，推进了从理论建模到硬件设计和重建算法的整个信号链。开发了一个基于物理的解析模型来描述复合材料中SFCW传播和多界面回波形成。在该模型指导下，通过集成矢量网络分析仪（VNA）、毫米波扩展模块和“4f”准光学聚焦系统，实现了一个模块化平台。此外，提出了一种自适应缺陷增强与相位辅助层析成像（ADEPT）算法，结合了基于相位的几何参考和基于参考的增强，以提取细微缺陷特征并支持3D重建。在75–110 GHz范围内的实验展示了泡沫芯夹层复合材料中的典型缺陷，实现了1 mm横向和0.5 mm轴向灵敏度、超过20 mm的穿透深度以及45 dB的动态范围。所提出的框架为毫米波SFCW成像及其在复合材料检测中的应用建立了物理一致且可扩展的基础。

## 基于单MIMO FMCW雷达的横向位移测量

原英文标题：Cross-range displacement measurement using a single MIMO FMCW radar

作者：Haibin Meng, Bo Li, Yuyong Xiong, Ming Li, Xin Zhou, Xi Liang, Zhike Peng

出版来源：Mechanical Systems and Signal Processing

出版时间：2026-04-30

原英文摘要：Displacement measurement using microwave radar has attracted extensive research interest. However, existing techniques are limited to detecting motion along the radial direction. This paper proposes a method for measuring cross-range displacement based on a single multiple-input multiple-output (MIMO) frequency modulated continuous wave (FMCW) radar, achieving for the first time the measurement of non-radial displacement using a single radar system. The proposed approach compensates for the phase variations induced by range and angle migration caused by cross-range motion, and decouples the phase components in the intermediate-frequency (IF) signal that are jointly dependent on target range and angle, thereby extracting the cross-range displacement. Rigorous theoretical analysis demonstrates the feasibility of the method, while simulation results confirm its effectiveness. Experimental results further verify that the proposed method can reliably measure both small-amplitude high-frequency displacements and large-amplitude low-frequency displacements.

中文摘要：使用微波雷达进行位移测量已引起广泛研究兴趣。然而，现有技术仅限于检测沿径向方向的运动。本文提出了一种基于单输入多输出（MIMO）调频连续波（FMCW）雷达测量横向位移的方法，首次实现了使用单个雷达系统测量非径向位移。所提方法补偿了由横向运动引起的距离和角度迁移所导致的相位变化，并解耦了中频（IF）信号中联合依赖于目标距离和角度的相位分量，从而提取横向位移。严格的理论分析证明了该方法的可行性，仿真结果证实了其有效性。实验结果进一步验证了所提方法能够可靠测量小幅高频位移和大幅低频位移。

原英文摘要：The increasing aging population has made fall detection a critical focus in intelligent health monitoring. Millimeter-wave radar has gained widespread attention in fall detection due to its non-contact operation and inherent privacy protection. However, it still faces challenges such as coordinate system representation, disjoint spatio-temporal features, and high data annotation costs. To address these issues, this study proposes an intelligent fall detection network named IFDNet. First, we propose a geometry-aware relative coordinate encoding scheme to replace conventional absolute coordinate system or voxel-based representations. This achieves geometric invariance against spatial displacements, enhancing the model's ability to detect subtle, fine-grained limb movements. Secondly, to combat the sparsity and flickering of radar data, we design a spatio-temporal joint modeling network that adaptively couples a dynamic Transformer gating mechanism with 3D residual blocks. Moreover, an adaptive semi-supervised learning strategy is proposed for data annotation. By dynamically adapting to the model's learning state, it overcomes the severe class imbalance typical of fixed-threshold methods, generating robust pseudo-labels. Experimental results demonstrate that IFDNet achieves an accuracy of 98.2% in the six-class task. Notably, this competitive performance is achieved using only 50% of the labeled data required by traditional methods. The introduction of IFDNet offers a highly robust and data-efficient solution for intelligent fall detection.
