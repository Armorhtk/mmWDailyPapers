## 面向 mmWave MIMO 信道估计的模型驱动深度展开 AMP 网络 [J]

原英文标题：A Model-Driven Deep-Unfolded AMP Network for mmWave MIMO Channel Estimation

作者：Yunshan Sun, Ruogu Chen, Ting Liu, Teng Fei, Yanqin Li, Xueyao Jiang

出版来源：Physical Communication

出版时间：2026-08-01

原英文摘要：To address high-dimensional channel estimation in millimeter-wave massive MIMO systems, we propose a model-driven unfolded reconstruction network that combines deep learning with iterative signal processing. By unrolling a conventional iterative estimator into a multi-layer architecture, the proposed method enables progressive refinement of beamspace channel estimates in a data-driven manner while retaining the interpretability of the underlying iterative updates. We further develop a multi-stage feature optimization module with dynamic path selection to integrate hierarchical representations and to fuse multi-scale features via attention-based weighting, which strengthens informative components and mitigates redundancy across branches. This adaptive fusion strategy enhances estimation robustness under model uncertainty and improves the quality of latent channel representations. A lightweight decoder subsequently maps the optimized latent features back to full channel states to produce the final estimate. Simulations under typical mmWave channel settings demonstrate consistent NMSE gains over representative iterative and deep-learning baselines across a wide SNR range, together with stable convergence behavior and favorable computational efficiency.

## 面向海上障碍物跟踪的航迹到航迹融合方法 [J]

原英文标题：A Track-to-track Fusion Method for Tracking Maritime Obstacles

作者：Ha-Yun Kim, Myung-Il Roh, Yeong-Min Jo, Hye-Won Lee, Yun-Sik Kim, In-Chang Yeo

出版来源：International Journal of Naval Architecture and Ocean Engineering

出版时间：2026-06-21

原英文摘要：Accurate tracking of maritime obstacles is essential to ensuring safe ship navigation. To this end, a variety of sensors such as AIS, RADAR, LiDAR, and cameras are widely employed. Data from these sensors is used to estimate the state of surrounding obstacles. However, each sensor has unique characteristics, including its detection range and measurement frequency, making it challenging to achieve stable and precise tracking with a single sensor. Therefore, a sensor fusion method for obstacle tracking is required to compensate for each sensor strengths and weaknesses. In this study, we proposed a track-to-track fusion method to improve the tracking accuracy of maritime obstacles by integrating RADAR and camera data. The RADAR data were processed using the CA-CFAR algorithm, and the camera data were processed using YOLOv11 to detect maritime obstacles. The EKF was then applied to the detection values from both sensors to track the maritime obstacles. These tracking results were then integrated using a track-to-track fusion method applying the WMA algorithm. Finally, the proposed method was validated across a range of maritime encounter scenarios using a virtual simulator that emulates the maritime environment. As a result, the track-to-track fusion method recorded mean tracking errors of 9.91 m for position, 10.88 degrees for COG, and 1.34 knots for SOG, relative to the single-sensor-based tracking method. These results indicate that the track-to-track fusion method effectively enhances the reliability of maritime obstacle tracking.

## 采用紧凑封装天线设计的全集成 mmWave MIMO 雷达 [C]

出版来源：2026 IEEE 76th Electronic Components and Technology Conference (ECTC)

出版时间：2026-05-26

原英文摘要：This paper presents a compact 10.9mm x 6.7mm millimeter-wave (mmWave) fully integrated radar device, leveraging flip-chip antenna-on-package (AoP) technology using a 4-layer cored substrate. The device utilizes Texas Instruments' radar integrated circuit, which features three receivers and two transmitters as active radio frequency front ends. The AoP design employs a novel cavity-backed, capacitive-fed patch antenna stacked with a parasitic meshed patch, achieving full 57GHz-64GHz bandwidth within the same substrate. The antenna placement is carefully optimized to follow a virtual multiple-input multiple-output array configuration, enabling a broad field of view. This paper discusses the detailed design methodology, including the matching of die impedance, use of electromagnetic bandgap structures to reduce antenna coupling, and optimization of the radiation pattern. The measured performance of this fully integrated device shows good correlation with simulations. Furthermore, an independent antenna isolation measurement technique is introduced, which is demonstrated through measurements, providing valuable insights into the device performance.

## 面向基础设施监测的毫米波 GB-ArcSAR 双频差分相位形变检测系统 [J]

原英文标题：Millimeter -Wave GB-ArcSAR System for Infrastructure Monitoring: Precise Deformation Detection via Dual-Band Differential Phase

作者：Yizhe Wang, Shuliang Gui, Yue Li, Kaikai Liu, Ze Li, Zengshan Tian

出版来源：IEEE Transactions on Microwave Theory and Techniques

出版时间：2026-06-19

原英文摘要：Millimeter-wave (MMW) ground-based interferometric synthetic aperture radar (GB-InSAR) plays an important role in monitoring microdeformations of large-scale infrastructure due to its strong anti-interference capability, low latency, and ease of deployment. In this study, a low-cost ground-based arc synthetic aperture radar (GB-ArcSAR) experimental platform was developed using an IWR6843 MMW radar chip, a turntable, and a control motor. However, achieving high-precision deformation detection in practical monitoring scenarios remains challenging because of the difficulty in selecting permanent scatterer points under complex far-field scattering conditions, the fluctuation of the initial signal phase caused by hardware instability, and the integer phase ambiguity introduced by the short wavelength of millimeter waves. To address these issues, this article proposes a high-precision and low-complexity deformation detection algorithm based on the differential phase of dual-band signals. Experimental results on buildings show that the proposed deformation detection algorithm enables the developed system to achieve submillimeter accuracy. Furthermore, experiments under different target materials and weather conditions reveal that the detection performance for metal iron plates is better than that for wooden boards. Under windy and foggy conditions, the deformation detection error is less than 0.2mm, while under rainy conditions, due to rain clutter, the error increases to within 0.8mm.

## OFDM 雷达目标检测：运动补偿的先跟踪后检测方案 [Preprint]

原英文标题：OFDM Radar Target Detection: A Motion-Compensated Track-Before-Detect Scheme

作者：Spyridon K. Chronopoulos, George P. Efthymoglou, Konstantinos P. Peppas

出版来源：Research Square

出版时间：2026-06-11

原英文摘要：Orthogonal frequency division multiplexing (OFDM) radar is increasingly under consideration for joint sensing and communication (JSAC) applications, due to its flexible subcarrier structure, efficient spectral use, and compatibility with modern communication signals (waveforms). In this work, a baseband OFDM radar framework is developed for moving-target detection, range-Doppler processing, and dynamic target tracking. The simulated system includes an OFDM radar response generated for predetermined subcarrier-symbol sets, while range-velocity data is extracted through Doppler processing. The main contribution is a motion-compensated track-before-detect/interacting multiple model (MC-TBD-IMM) tracking scheme. Unlike conventional constant false alarm rate (CFAR) based approaches, the proposed method integrates target-associated range-Doppler detection information over time in the region where the target is predicted to appear. Before each integration step, the target-related information from previous frames is shifted based on the predicted target motion, reducing the influence of detections from older target positions. The proposed method is compared against two baseline tracking schemes. These are CFAR combined with a Kalman filter (CFAR-KF) and CFAR combined with an interacting multiple model (IMM) tracker (CFAR-IMM). The validation is performed through four OFDM radar simulation sets, covering 500 MHz and 1000 MHz bandwidth configurations with 256/512 OFDM symbols. Simulation results show that the proposed MC-TBD-IMM method provides more accurate range and velocity estimates than the CFAR-KF and CFAR-IMM baselines.

出版时间：2026-06-27

原英文摘要：This paper presents PULSE, a portable, low-cost radar sensing node developed to enable rapid and reliable human detection in obstructed and hazardous environments, with a primary focus on search-and-rescue operations. PULSE integrates a 24 GHz Frequency Modulated Continuous Wave (FMCW) millimeter-wave radar module with a Raspberry Pi 4, which handles signal acquisition, basic processing, and wireless communication. The prototype maintains stable wireless transmission and low-latency visualization suitable for field deployment scenarios. The results highlight the feasibility of low-cost radar platforms for scalable disaster-response applications, offering a compact and cost-effective alternative to other emergency life-detection systems.

## RadSyn：通过视频与文本融合合成大规模真实 mmWave 雷达训练数据 [J]

原英文标题：RadSyn: Synthesizing Large-scale Realistic mmWave Radar Training Data via Video and Text Fusion

作者：Kaikai Deng, Yue Ling, Ling Xing, Zijian Cao, Honghai Wu, Huahong Ma

出版来源：IEEE Transactions on Cognitive Communications and Networking

出版时间：2026-06-17

原英文摘要：Millimeter-wave radar-based gesture recognition has emerged as a promising paradigm for human-computer interaction, with applications spanning diverse domains. Nevertheless, the development of robust and generalizable deep learning models in these areas remains hindered by the limited availability of large-scale training datasets, particularly in scenes involving lying postures. Existing radar data synthesis approaches are often inadequate, largely due to the scarcity and narrow scope of available data sources. To this end, we propose RadSyn, a novel system designed to synthesize large-scale, realistic radar data from either video or textual inputs. The central contribution of RadSyn lies in its ability to establish a reliable cross-modal mapping from semantic representations to radar signals. The system comprises four core components: a video feature encoding network, a text feature encoding network, a radar feature encoding network, and a data fitting and decoding network that leverages a variational autoencoder-based mechanism to align latent distributions and synthesize realistic radar data at scale. Experimental results demonstrate that, when training the gesture recognition model solely on the synthetic radar data, RadSyn achieves an accuracy improvement of 32.88% over the state-of-the-art method.

## 基于相机与 4D 毫米波雷达融合的三维目标检测算法研究 [J]

原英文标题：Research on a Three-Dimensional Object Detection Algorithm Based on Camera and 4D Millimeter-Wave Radar Fusion

作者：Zhikui Lu, Qimin Xu, Benwu Wang, He Zhu

出版来源：Ingegneria Sismica

出版时间：2026-06-10

原英文摘要：In order to solve the problem of low precision and shortage of space information in single sensor system, a new method of 3D object detection based on 4D MMW radar is presented. The proposed method combines multi-modal feature fusion and spatiotemporal scaling, and adopts a middle level fusion strategy to model multi-element features of radar point cloud. The camera branch uses the ResNet-FPN architecture to extract multiscale semantic features, and the radar branch uses a VoxelNet-based compression structure to improve the performance of the algorithm. Experiments on the 4D-Drive and nuScenes data sets show that the proposed algorithm achieves 86.4% detection accuracy when the IoU threshold is 0.7. Compared with the single-modality baseline, there is a 12.8% increase in performance compared to a camera only mode, and 9.5% in the radar-only mode. Compared with the monocular vision approach, the absolute displacement error has been decreased to 0.184 m, and the detection accuracy remains above 85% under challenging conditions, showing strong robustness and generalization ability. The system achieves real time performance at 31 FPS on the RTX A6000 and Jetson AGX Orin platforms. This study has overcome the shortcomings of the existing detection algorithms, such as precision, latency, and environment adaptability, and provides an efficient way to realize multi-source collaboration.

## 基于 FMCW 雷达与稀疏贝叶斯自适应滤波的鲁棒呼吸率和心率估计 [J]

原英文标题：Robust respiratory and heart rate estimation via FMCW radar and sparse Bayesian adaptive filtering

作者：Pengwei Wen, Jianpeng Zheng, Boyang Qu, Xuzhao Chai, Li Yan, Sheng Zhang

出版来源：Biomedical Signal Processing and Control

出版时间：2026-10-15

原英文摘要：Non-contact vital sign detection, particularly for respiratory rate and heart rate, shows significant potential in healthcare applications due to its comfort advantages and privacy preservation. Nevertheless, detecting weak heartbeat signals remains challenging because of respiratory harmonics, ambient noise, and environmental clutter. To address this challenge, we propose a vital signs detection system based on frequency-modulated continuous wave radar. The system is built around a streamlined framework that combines four core modules designed to accurately extract respiratory rate and heart rate from the radar signals. The proposed system begins by removing static clutter using a Moving Target Detection algorithm within the preprocessing module. Following this, the vital sign extraction module performs noncoherent integration to identify the distance bins most relevant to physiological activity. In the signal enhancement module, Pearson correlation analysis is used to combine signal regions with high consistency, while first-order phase differencing is applied to suppress baseline drift. This improves the stability and reliability of the extracted signals. To isolate respiratory and heart signals, the system employs a wavelet packet decomposition with autocorrelation algorithm. Finally, a sparse Bayesian least-mean-squares algorithm is introduced to dynamically refine prior information. The experimental results show that the system achieves mean absolute errors of 0.53 Bpm for respiratory rate and 0.93 Bpm for heart rate, demonstrating its notable capability in suppressing noise and respiratory harmonic interference. Furthermore, extensive experiments conducted in realistic office environments show that, compared with existing methods, the system exhibits excellent robustness across different users, distances, and angles conditions.

## 基于扩散与流匹配的特定场景 MIMO 信道生成：保真度、效率与下游效用 [Preprint]

原英文标题：Site-Specific MIMO Channel Generation via Diffusion and Flow Matching: Fidelity, Efficiency, and Downstream Utility

作者：Sina Beyraghi, Masoud Sadeghian, Firdous Bin Ismail, Angel Lozano, Paul Almasan, Giovanni Geraci

出版来源：arXiv

出版时间：2026-06-18

原英文摘要：This paper explores the use of generative models to synthesize high-quality, site-specific multiple-input multiple-output (MIMO) channel data, addressing the high cost of the extensive measurement campaigns required to acquire real-world data for AI-native wireless networks. Two location-conditioned generative paradigms are compared: a conditional denoising diffusion implicit model (cDDIM), and a conditional flow matching model (cFMM). Both these models generate MIMO channel matrices conditioned on user coordinates, to preserve the spatial structure of the deployment site. The approaches are evaluated across three dimensions: statistical fidelity (including beam consistency and effective rank), generation efficiency, and utility in downstream tasks such as channel-state information compression and beam alignment. Results across diverse propagation scenarios (28 GHz and 3.5 GHz, both line-of-sight and non-line-of-sight) demonstrate that both models accurately capture site-specific characteristics, even when trained on scarce ground-truth data. Notably, cFMM achieves a quality comparable to cDDIM with roughly an order of magnitude less inference time. Augmenting scarce site-specific datasets with these synthetic channels yields hefty performance gains in downstream physical layer tasks compared to using scarce data alone or stochastic channels.

## 使用 AWR1843BOOST 雷达在海杂波环境中跟踪单目标 [J]

原英文标题：Tracking single target in the presence of sea clutter using AWR1843BOOST radar

作者：Manjukiran B, Shantharama Rai C, Gnane swarnadh Satapathi, Suhandas K

出版来源：Engineering Research Express

出版时间：2026-06-19

原英文摘要：This work provides the first detailed system-level, field experiment based comparison of single target maritime tracking using the AWR1843BOOST 77 GHz FMCW radar, at two test locations near Mangaluru, India, with four distinctly different scenarios involving variation in tide level, target maneuver and vessel speed (5-15m/s). Four well established single target tracking algorithms, Probability Hypothesis Density (PHD), Joint Probabilistic Data Association (JPDA), Global Nearest Neighbor (GNN) and Particle Filter (PF) are evaluated under identical hardware, system level preprocessing conditions and providing a standardized and reproducible comparison, not previously reported for a low cost mmWave system at a coastal canal. Signal to clutter ratio is improved by 12 dB by using doppler filtering followed by CFAR detection. Vessel location estimates are obtained from independent GPS measurements sampled at 2 sec interval as ground truth data. The sea clutter was modeled as a spatially homogeneous Poisson process with intensity lambda_c = 0.1 det/m^2 (high tide) fitted empirically on K-distribution statistics measured at each test site. Algorithm performance was evaluated over ten Monte Carlo simulations using measures: position RMSE, velocity RMSE, FAR, Track Continuity Ratio (TCR), Signal to Clutter Ratio (SCR), Track Initiation Time (TIT), Track Loss Rate (TLR), Clutter Rejection Rate (CRR), Robustness to Occlusions (RTO), and Energy Efficiency (EE) with statistical significance confirmed via one way ANOVA and Tukey HSD post-hoc comparison test. PHD provides best overall performance for the low clutter scenario 3, whereas PF provides the best velocity estimation and TCR for the fast maneuvering target in scenario 4 at the cost of higher processing load.

## Viking Hill Dataset：面向森林场景检测与分割的 LiDAR-雷达-相机数据集 [Preprint]

原英文标题：Viking Hill Dataset: A Lidar-Radar-Camera Dataset for Detection and Segmentation in Forest Scenes

作者：Vladimír Kubelka, Oleksandr Kotlyar, Unal Artan, Martin Magnusson

出版来源：arXiv

出版时间：2026-06-17

原英文摘要：Autonomous robots operating under forest canopies need robust perception of trees and surrounding vegetation across varying seasonal conditions. Existing forestry datasets provide lidar or camera data with per-tree annotations, but none include co-registered 4D imaging radar -- a modality of growing interest for its resilience to visual degradation, surface contamination, and vegetation occlusion. We introduce a multi-sensor forest dataset collected by a mobile robot equipped with a high-resolution FMCW imaging radar, lidar, RGB camera, IMU, and RTK-GNSS. The site was recorded in two sessions under contrasting vegetation states, and 3D cuboid annotations -- including per-tree diameter estimates -- provide shared semantic labels across all three perception modalities. Furthermore, we provide baseline results for semantic segmentation of the radar and lidar point clouds using MinkowskiUNet. Radar achieves IoU scores competitive with lidar for dominant classes (ground 91%, canopy 86%) while lagging on geometrically fine structures such as tree trunks (56% vs. 74%). A cross-modality analysis further compares lidar and radar trunk segmentation against an RGB detection model, and a diameter-stratified evaluation reveals how trunk segmentation quality varies with tree size. Beyond segmentation, the co-registered multi-modal data and RTK-GNSS-aided reference positioning support research in mapping, localization, and sensor fusion under canopy. The dataset and annotation tools are publicly available.
