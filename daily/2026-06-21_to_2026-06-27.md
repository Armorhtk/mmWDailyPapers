## 面向 mmWave MIMO 信道估计的模型驱动深度展开 AMP 网络 [J]

原英文标题：A Model-Driven Deep-Unfolded AMP Network for mmWave MIMO Channel Estimation

作者：Yunshan Sun, Ruogu Chen, Ting Liu, Teng Fei, Yanqin Li, Xueyao Jiang

出版来源：Physical Communication

出版时间：2026-08-01

原英文摘要：To address high-dimensional channel estimation in millimeter-wave massive MIMO systems, we propose a model-driven unfolded reconstruction network that combines deep learning with iterative signal processing. By unrolling a conventional iterative estimator into a multi-layer architecture, the proposed method enables progressive refinement of beamspace channel estimates in a data-driven manner while retaining the interpretability of the underlying iterative updates. We further develop a multi-stage feature optimization module with dynamic path selection to integrate hierarchical representations and to fuse multi-scale features via attention-based weighting, which strengthens informative components and mitigates redundancy across branches. This adaptive fusion strategy enhances estimation robustness under model uncertainty and improves the quality of latent channel representations. A lightweight decoder subsequently maps the optimized latent features back to full channel states to produce the final estimate. Simulations under typical mmWave channel settings demonstrate consistent NMSE gains over representative iterative and deep-learning baselines across a wide SNR range, together with stable convergence behavior and favorable computational efficiency.

中文摘要：本文面向毫米波大规模 MIMO 系统中的高维信道估计问题，提出一种模型驱动的深度展开 AMP 重构网络。方法将传统迭代估计器展开为多层网络，在保留迭代更新可解释性的同时，以数据驱动方式逐步细化波束域信道估计。作者进一步设计了带动态路径选择的多阶段特征优化模块，通过注意力加权融合多尺度特征，强化有效信息并减少冗余分支，从而提升模型不确定性下的估计鲁棒性。最后，轻量解码器将优化后的潜在特征映射回完整信道状态。典型 mmWave 信道仿真表明，该方法在较宽 SNR 范围内相对代表性迭代和深度学习基线取得稳定 NMSE 增益，并具备较好的收敛性和计算效率。

## 面向海上障碍物跟踪的航迹到航迹融合方法 [J]

原英文标题：A Track-to-track Fusion Method for Tracking Maritime Obstacles

作者：Ha-Yun Kim, Myung-Il Roh, Yeong-Min Jo, Hye-Won Lee, Yun-Sik Kim, In-Chang Yeo

出版来源：International Journal of Naval Architecture and Ocean Engineering

出版时间：2026-06-21

原英文摘要：Accurate tracking of maritime obstacles is essential to ensuring safe ship navigation. To this end, a variety of sensors such as AIS, RADAR, LiDAR, and cameras are widely employed. Data from these sensors is used to estimate the state of surrounding obstacles. However, each sensor has unique characteristics, including its detection range and measurement frequency, making it challenging to achieve stable and precise tracking with a single sensor. Therefore, a sensor fusion method for obstacle tracking is required to compensate for each sensor strengths and weaknesses. In this study, we proposed a track-to-track fusion method to improve the tracking accuracy of maritime obstacles by integrating RADAR and camera data. The RADAR data were processed using the CA-CFAR algorithm, and the camera data were processed using YOLOv11 to detect maritime obstacles. The EKF was then applied to the detection values from both sensors to track the maritime obstacles. These tracking results were then integrated using a track-to-track fusion method applying the WMA algorithm. Finally, the proposed method was validated across a range of maritime encounter scenarios using a virtual simulator that emulates the maritime environment. As a result, the track-to-track fusion method recorded mean tracking errors of 9.91 m for position, 10.88 degrees for COG, and 1.34 knots for SOG, relative to the single-sensor-based tracking method. These results indicate that the track-to-track fusion method effectively enhances the reliability of maritime obstacle tracking.

中文摘要：本文针对海上障碍物稳定跟踪问题，提出一种融合 RADAR 与相机数据的航迹到航迹融合方法。雷达侧使用 CA-CFAR 检测，视觉侧使用 YOLOv11 识别海上障碍物，再分别用扩展卡尔曼滤波估计目标状态，最后通过加权移动平均策略融合两类传感器航迹。作者在可模拟多种海上会遇场景的虚拟仿真环境中验证方法。结果显示，相比单传感器跟踪基线，航迹融合方法在位置、航向和速度估计上均降低了平均跟踪误差，说明该方法能够利用不同传感器的互补特性，提高海上障碍物跟踪的可靠性。

原文链接：https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S2092678226000476

This paper discusses the detailed design methodology, including the matching of die impedance, use of electromagnetic bandgap structures to reduce antenna coupling, and optimization of the radiation pattern. The measured performance of this fully integrated device shows good correlation with simulations. Furthermore, an independent antenna isolation measurement technique is introduced, which is demonstrated through measurements, providing valuable insights into the device performance.

原英文摘要：Millimeter-wave (MMW) ground-based interferometric synthetic aperture radar (GB-InSAR) plays an important role in monitoring microdeformations of large-scale infrastructure due to its strong anti-interference capability, low latency, and ease of deployment. In this study, a low-cost ground-based arc synthetic aperture radar (GB-ArcSAR) experimental platform was developed using an IWR6843 MMW radar chip, a turntable, and a control motor. However, achieving high-precision deformation detection in practical monitoring scenarios remains challenging because of the difficulty in selecting permanent scatterer points under complex far-field scattering conditions, the fluctuation of the initial signal phase caused by hardware instability, and the integer phase ambiguity introduced by the short wavelength of millimeter waves. To address these issues, this article proposes a high-precision and low-complexity deformation detection algorithm based on the differential phase of dual-band signals. Experimental results on buildings show that the proposed deformation detection algorithm enables the developed system to achieve submillimeter accuracy. Furthermore, experiments under different target materials and weather conditions reveal that the detection performance for metal iron plates is better than that for wooden boards. ## OFDM 雷达目标检测：运动补偿的先跟踪后检测方案 [Preprint]

原英文标题：OFDM Radar Target Detection: A Motion-Compensated Track-Before-Detect Scheme

作者：Spyridon K. Chronopoulos, George P. Efthymoglou, Konstantinos P. Peppas

出版来源：Research Square

出版时间：2026-06-11

原英文摘要：Orthogonal frequency division multiplexing (OFDM) radar is increasingly under consideration for joint sensing and communication (JSAC) applications, due to its flexible subcarrier structure, efficient spectral use, and compatibility with modern communication signals (waveforms). In this work, a baseband OFDM radar framework is developed for moving-target detection, range-Doppler processing, and dynamic target tracking. The simulated system includes an OFDM radar response generated for predetermined subcarrier-symbol sets, while range-velocity data is extracted through Doppler processing. The main contribution is a motion-compensated track-before-detect/interacting multiple model (MC-TBD-IMM) tracking scheme. Unlike conventional constant false alarm rate (CFAR) based approaches, the proposed method integrates target-associated range-Doppler detection information over time in the region where the target is predicted to appear. Before each integration step, the target-related information from previous frames is shifted based on the predicted target motion, reducing the influence of detections from older target positions. The proposed method is compared against two baseline tracking schemes. These are CFAR combined with a Kalman filter (CFAR-KF) and CFAR combined with an interacting multiple model (IMM) tracker (CFAR-IMM). The validation is performed through four OFDM radar simulation sets, covering 500 MHz and 1000 MHz bandwidth configurations with 256/512 OFDM symbols. Simulation results show that the proposed MC-TBD-IMM method provides more accurate range and velocity estimates than the CFAR-KF and CFAR-IMM baselines.

中文摘要：本文研究 OFDM 雷达在联合感知与通信场景中的运动目标检测和动态跟踪问题。作者构建了一个基带 OFDM 雷达框架，用于运动目标检测、距离-多普勒处理和目标跟踪，并提出运动补偿的先跟踪后检测/交互多模型方法 MC-TBD-IMM。不同于传统基于 CFAR 的方法，该方法在目标预测区域内随时间累积与目标相关的距离-多普勒检测信息，并在每次累积前根据预测运动对历史信息进行移位，以减少旧目标位置检测对当前估计的影响。四组 OFDM 雷达仿真覆盖 500 MHz 和 1000 MHz 带宽以及 256/512 个 OFDM 符号配置。结果表明，MC-TBD-IMM 相比 CFAR-KF 和 CFAR-IMM 基线能够获得更低的距离、速度和归一化跟踪误差。

Existing radar data synthesis approaches are often inadequate, largely due to the scarcity and narrow scope of available data sources. ## 基于 FMCW 雷达与稀疏贝叶斯自适应滤波的鲁棒呼吸率和心率估计 [J]

作者：Pengwei Wen, Jianpeng Zheng, Boyang Qu, Xuzhao Chai, Li Yan, Sheng Zhang

出版来源：Biomedical Signal Processing and Control

出版时间：2026-10-15

原英文摘要：Non-contact vital sign detection, particularly for respiratory rate and heart rate, shows significant potential in healthcare applications due to its comfort advantages and privacy preservation. Nevertheless, detecting weak heartbeat signals remains challenging because of respiratory harmonics, ambient noise, and environmental clutter. To address this challenge, we propose a vital signs detection system based on frequency-modulated continuous wave radar. The system is built around a streamlined framework that combines four core modules designed to accurately extract respiratory rate and heart rate from the radar signals. The proposed system begins by removing static clutter using a Moving Target Detection algorithm within the preprocessing module. Following this, the vital sign extraction module performs noncoherent integration to identify the distance bins most relevant to physiological activity. In the signal enhancement module, Pearson correlation analysis is used to combine signal regions with high consistency, while first-order phase differencing is applied to suppress baseline drift. This improves the stability and reliability of the extracted signals. To isolate respiratory and heart signals, the system employs a wavelet packet decomposition with autocorrelation algorithm. Finally, a sparse Bayesian least-mean-squares algorithm is introduced to dynamically refine prior information. The experimental results show that the system achieves mean absolute errors of 0.53 Bpm for respiratory rate and 0.93 Bpm for heart rate, demonstrating its notable capability in suppressing noise and respiratory harmonic interference. Furthermore, extensive experiments conducted in realistic office environments show that, compared with existing methods, the system exhibits excellent robustness across different users, distances, and angles conditions.

## 基于扩散与流匹配的特定场景 MIMO 信道生成：保真度、效率与下游效用 [Preprint]

原英文标题：Site-Specific MIMO Channel Generation via Diffusion and Flow Matching: Fidelity, Efficiency, and Downstream Utility

作者：Sina Beyraghi, Masoud Sadeghian, Firdous Bin Ismail, Angel Lozano, Paul Almasan, Giovanni Geraci

出版来源：arXiv

出版时间：2026-06-18

原英文摘要：This paper explores the use of generative models to synthesize high-quality, site-specific multiple-input multiple-output (MIMO) channel data, addressing the high cost of the extensive measurement campaigns required to acquire real-world data for AI-native wireless networks. Two location-conditioned generative paradigms are compared: a conditional denoising diffusion implicit model (cDDIM), and a conditional flow matching model (cFMM). Both these models generate MIMO channel matrices conditioned on user coordinates, to preserve the spatial structure of the deployment site. The approaches are evaluated across three dimensions: statistical fidelity (including beam consistency and effective rank), generation efficiency, and utility in downstream tasks such as channel-state information compression and beam alignment. Results across diverse propagation scenarios (28 GHz and 3.5 GHz, both line-of-sight and non-line-of-sight) demonstrate that both models accurately capture site-specific characteristics, even when trained on scarce ground-truth data.
