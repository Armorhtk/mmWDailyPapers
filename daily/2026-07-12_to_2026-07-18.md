## 4DR360：面向4D雷达-相机全场景感知中联合3D检测与占据预测的状态推理 [Preprint]

原英文标题：4DR360: State Reasoning for Joint 3D Detection and Occupancy Prediction in 4D Radar-Camera Full-Scene Perception

作者：Xiaokai Bai, Lianqing Zheng, Runwei Guan, Songkai Wang, Siyuan Cao, Hui-liang Shen

出版来源：arXiv

出版时间：2026-07-10

原英文摘要：Reliable autonomous driving requires full-scene perception that couples foreground objects with dense semantic layout. Recently, 4D millimeter-wave radar has emerged as a robust and affordable sensor, yet its sparse returns make radar-camera fusion necessary for comprehensive scene understanding. Existing radar-camera methods mainly optimize detection, while dual-task systems usually decode boxes and occupancy with limited interaction. To address this gap and advance radar-based multi-task learning, we propose \method, a 4D radar-camera framework for 360$^\circ$ full-scene perception, which models semantic occupancy as a persistent scene state rather than a terminal output. \method{} follows a cross-modal state reasoning paradigm, where the occupancy state is modeled and propagated through stages for coarse-to-fine feature aggregation. Specifically, State-guided BEV Enhancement (SBE) strengthens intra-frame BEV representation, while Doppler-guided Temporal Fusion (DTF) preserves state evidence over longer temporal horizons. Beyond the model, we further extend ManTruckScenes with satellite-map-based generated occupancy labels and pair it with OmniHD-Scenes in a unified cross-dataset detection-and-occupancy protocol. The resulting experiments cover accuracy, robustness, ablation, and efficiency under one radar-camera multi-task evaluation framework. Code and labels will be released upon acceptance.

中文摘要：可靠的自动驾驶需要全场景感知，将前景对象与密集的语义布局耦合起来。近年来，4D毫米波雷达作为一种鲁棒且价格合理的传感器出现，但其稀疏回波使得雷达-相机融合对于全面场景理解必不可少。现有雷达-相机方法主要优化检测，而双任务系统通常以有限的交互解码框和占据。为弥补这一差距并推进基于雷达的多任务学习，我们提出4DR360，一种用于360°全场景感知的4D雷达-相机框架，它将语义占据建模为持久的场景状态而非终端输出。4DR360遵循跨模态状态推理范式，其中占据状态被建模并通过阶段传播以实现从粗到细的特征聚合。具体来说，状态引导的BEV增强（SBE）加强了帧内BEV表示，而行多普勒引导的时间融合（DTF）在更长的时间范围内保留状态证据。除了模型之外，我们还用基于卫星地图生成的占据标签扩展了ManTruckScenes，并将其与OmniHD-Scenes配对，形成一个统一的跨数据集检测与占据协议。由此产生的实验涵盖了在一个雷达-相机多任务评估框架下的准确性、鲁棒性、消融和效率。代码和标签将在论文接收后发布。

## 用于基于深度学习的隐蔽武器检测的平衡多模态热成像与被动毫米波数据集 [J]

原英文标题：A BALANCED MULTI-MODAL THERMAL AND PASSIVE MILLIMETER -WAVE DATASET FOR DEEP LEARNING-BASED CONCEALED WEAPON DETECTION

作者：S. Akinbohun, S. T. Apeh, I. A. Edeoghon

出版来源：International Journal of Renewable Energy and Environment

出版时间：2026-05-15

原英文摘要：In the field of smart city security surveillance, automated concealed weapon detection using non-invasive imaging remains a challenge. However, no publicly available dataset provides balanced, paired, and pre-processed thermal infrared (TIR) and passive millimeter-wave (PMMW) imagery specifically designed for training deep learning models and facilitating fair cross-modality comparison. The present study aimed to curate and standardize a balanced, multimodal dataset that enables the systematic evaluation of detection algorithms under controlled conditions. A dataset was designed that comprised 686 images from each of the secondary Thermal Infrared (TIR) and Passive Millimeter Wave (PMMW) modalities, using the FLIR Breach PTQ136 camera and Ka-band radiometer of 34 GHz frequency, respectively. The images were subjected to image flipping, rotation, scaling to a size of 224 x 224 pixels, normalizing to [0, 1] scale, and splitting into stratified partitions (70:20:10). This led to balanced binary classes with an augmentation of 1.72x for the TIR modality. Thus, the study curated a balanced dataset of 1,372 thermal and millimeter-wave images. Results show successful class parity and standardized preprocessing. Recommendations include collecting synchronized image pairs and expanding to outdoor environments.

中文摘要：在智慧城市安防监控领域，利用非侵入式成像进行自动隐蔽武器检测仍然是一个挑战。然而，目前尚无公开数据集提供专门为训练深度学习模型和促进公平跨模态比较而设计的平衡、配对且预处理的热红外（TIR）和被动毫米波（PMMW）图像。本研究旨在策划并标准化一个平衡的多模态数据集，以便在受控条件下系统评估检测算法。该数据集包含来自热红外（TIR）和被动毫米波（PMMW）两种模态各686张图像，分别使用FLIR Breach PTQ136相机和34 GHz频段的Ka波段辐射计采集。图像经过翻转、旋转、缩放至224×224像素、归一化到[0,1]范围，并分层划分为训练集、验证集和测试集（比例70:20:10）。由此产生了平衡的二分类，且TIR模态数据扩充了1.72倍。因此，本研究策划了一个包含1372张热成像和毫米波图像的平衡数据集。结果显示成功实现了类别平衡和标准化预处理。建议包括收集同步图像对并扩展到室外环境。

## 基于压缩感知的毫米波近场欠采样光栅扫描成像方法 [J]

原英文标题：A Compressive Sensing-Based Millimeter-Wave Near-Field Imaging Method for Undersampled Raster Scan Sampling

作者：Ruixiang Fang, Xiaojin Shi, Yunhua Zhang

出版来源：IEEE Sensors Letters

出版时间：2026-07-13

原英文摘要：Synthetic aperture radar-based millimeter-wave (MMW) near-field imaging technology has been widely adopted for its low cost and easy implementation, yet its scanning process is time consuming. The undersampled raster scan (URS) sampling can greatly shorten sampling time, thereby significantly improving imaging efficiency in MMW near-field imaging application. However, it inevitably causes image aliasing in the corresponding undersampling direction. To mitigate this aliasing, the azimuth compression based compressive sensing (AC-CS) method for URS sampling is proposed in this letter. To achieve successful CS reconstruction, the proposed method begins with a matrix separation process and performs data recovery based on AC-CS. An alias-free image can be obtained by applying the matched filtering method to the recovered matrix. Experimental results demonstrate that the proposed method achieves satisfactory imaging performance using only 30% of the fully-sampled raster scan sampled data, enabling a 70% reduction in scan time.

中文摘要：基于合成孔径雷达的毫米波近场成像技术因其成本低、易于实现而被广泛采用，但其扫描过程耗时。欠采样光栅扫描采样可大幅缩短采样时间，从而显著提高毫米波近场成像应用的成像效率。然而，它不可避免地会在欠采样方向上导致图像混叠。为了减轻这种混叠，本文提出了一种用于欠采样光栅扫描采样的方位压缩结合压缩感知方法。为了实现成功的压缩感知重建，该方法首先进行矩阵分离处理，并基于AC-CS进行数据恢复。通过对恢复后的矩阵应用匹配滤波方法，可以获得无混叠图像。实验结果表明，该方法仅使用全采样光栅扫描数据的30%即可获得令人满意的成像性能，扫描时间减少70%。

原文链接：https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/abstract/document/11606155

## 基于二阶Keystone变换的交通雷达运动车辆快速成像方法 [J]

原英文标题：A fast imaging method for moving vehicles in traffic radar based on second-order Keystone transform

作者：Wenjie Shen, Xiaotong Yin, Yanping Wang, Yun Lin, Yang Li, Zechao Bai, Xueyong Xu, Kuai Yu

出版来源：Digital Signal Processing

出版时间：2026-11-15

原英文摘要：Millimeter-wave traffic surveillance radars can detect moving vehicles and measure their speed, playing a crucial role in traffic supervision applications. However, these radars cannot image moving vehicles. Integrating radar imaging technology enables the acquisition of vehicle shape information for classification and recognition, thereby boosting their functional capability. Inverse synthetic aperture radar (ISAR) achieves two-dimensional high-resolution imaging of moving targets, yet traditional ISAR methods suffer from high algorithm complexity, heavy computational load and poor real-time performance, making them unsuitable for time-sensitive traffic scenarios. Given that existing traffic surveillance radars operate in high-frequency bands, sub-aperture-based processing maintains high resolution while reducing imaging algorithm complexity. Thus, this paper proposes a fast imaging method for moving vehicles in traffic surveillance radars based on the second-order Keystone transform. It first analyzes and constructs the radar’s observation geometry and sub-aperture signal model, then defines sub-aperture segmentation parameters with moving target imaging focus quality as the criterion. The method processes received echo data frame by frame to extract the target azimuth angle corresponding to each pulse, implements sub-aperture segmentation, adopts the second-order Keystone transform for range cell migration (RCM) correction within each sub-aperture, and performs range Inverse fast fourier transform (IFFT), azimuth fast fourier transform (FFT), and polar-to-Cartesian coordinate conversion to obtain 2D high-resolution vehicle images. The effectiveness of the proposed method is verified via moving vehicle point-target simulations and real data experiments.

中文摘要：毫米波交通监视雷达可以检测运动车辆并测量其速度，在交通监管应用中发挥着关键作用。然而，这些雷达无法对运动车辆成像。集成雷达成像技术能够获取车辆形状信息以进行分类和识别，从而增强其功能。逆合成孔径雷达（ISAR）可以实现运动目标的二维高分辨率成像，但传统ISAR方法算法复杂度高、计算量大、实时性差，不适用于对时间敏感的交通场景。考虑到现有交通监视雷达工作在高频段，基于子孔径的处理在降低成像算法复杂度的同时保持了高分辨率。因此，本文提出了一种基于二阶Keystone变换的交通监视雷达运动车辆快速成像方法。该方法首先分析并构建雷达的观测几何和子孔径信号模型，然后以运动目标成像聚焦质量为准则定义子孔径分割参数。方法逐帧处理接收到的回波数据，提取每个脉冲对应的目标方位角，实现子孔径分割，在每个子孔径内采用二阶Keystone变换进行距离单元徙动（RCM）校正，并进行距离向逆快速傅里叶变换（IFFT）、方位向快速傅里叶变换（FFT）以及极坐标到笛卡尔坐标转换，获得二维高分辨率车辆图像。通过运动车辆点目标仿真和实测数据实验验证了所提方法的有效性。

原文链接：https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1051200426005130

## CRFF-Net：基于超分辨率的4D毫米波雷达与相机跨模态融合用于3D目标检测 [J]

原英文标题：CRFF-Net: cross-modal fusion of 4D mmWave radar and camera with super-resolution for 3D object detection

作者：Aljohani, Abeer

出版来源：Journal of King Saud University Computer and Information Sciences

出版时间：2026-07-09

原英文摘要：Accurate 3D object detection is fundamental for autonomous driving systems, but existing state-of-the-art methods are yet to overcome challenges in terms of achieving consistent and robust results. The two main difficulties in multimodal perception are the sparse nature of 4D mm-wave radar point clouds, and the modality gap between radar and camera features for fusion in a common representation space. Such problems can adversely affect the accuracy of detection, especially for small and far away objects. To cope with these problems, this paper presents an Adaptive Feature Alignment Cross-modal Radar-camera Fusion Framework (CRFF-Net) based on radar point cloud super-resolution. A spatial module based on Graph Convolutional Network (GCN) is proposed to enhance the spatial representation of radar point clouds by producing denser and geometrically consistent ones. Moreover, a deformable cross-attention mechanism is introduced to match the features of radar and image in the Bird's Eye View (BEV) space adaptively, which can alleviate the loss of information that occurs in traditional fusion methods. The result indicates that CRFF-Net has a mean Average Precision (mAP) of 57.43%, which is superior to the strong radar-camera fusion basis, LXL, by 1.12%, and the result is verified by extensive experiments on the View-of-Delft (VoD) data set. The proposed method also demonstrates its robustness and maintains high detection performance under noisy conditions compared to the existing methods. The findings suggest that the adaptive cross-modal fusion of the super-resolution radar data and the video image can effectively and reliably detect the 3D shape of the object in complex driving environments.

中文摘要：精确的3D目标检测是自动驾驶系统的基础，但现有最先进方法在获得一致且鲁棒的结果方面仍面临挑战。多模态感知的两个主要难点是4D毫米波雷达点云的稀疏性，以及雷达与相机特征在共同表示空间中融合时的模态差异。这些问题可能对检测精度产生不利影响，尤其是对小而远的物体。为了解决这些问题，本文提出了一种基于雷达点云超分辨率的自适应特征对齐跨模态雷达-相机融合框架（CRFF-Net）。提出了一种基于图卷积网络（GCN）的空间模块，通过生成更密集且几何一致的点云来增强雷达点云的空间表示。此外，引入了一种可变形交叉注意力机制，在鸟瞰图（BEV）空间中自适应地匹配雷达和图像的特征，这可以缓解传统融合方法中发生的信息丢失。结果表明，CRFF-Net的平均精度（mAP）为57.43%，比强大的雷达-相机融合基准LXL高出1.12%，并在View-of-Delft（VoD）数据集上进行了广泛实验验证。与现有方法相比，所提出的方法还展示了其鲁棒性，并在噪声条件下保持了高检测性能。研究结果表明，超分辨率雷达数据与视频图像的自适应跨模态融合能够在复杂驾驶环境中有效且可靠地检测物体的3D形状。

## 高效嵌入式毫米波人体姿态估计 [M]

原英文标题：Efficient Embedded mmWave Human Pose Estimation

作者：Robin Verver

出版来源：Delft University of Technology

出版时间：2026-06-21

原英文摘要：Human-pose estimation is a technology with many applications such as for healthcare, smart-homes and new methods of human-computer interaction. However, traditional RGB camera-based systems come at significant privacy risks and can perform poorly in dark rooms. A new approach to human-pose estimation, estimating through the use of mmWave radars, could solve these problems. MmWave creates a pointcloud of a person, rather than a direct RGB image and is therefore not affected by dark conditions, while simultaneously letting the subject stay anonymous. Current mmWave models are very accurate, on the order of centimetres, but generally too costly to run without a GPU. In this paper we create an optimised mmWave human-pose estimation model that runs more accurately without a GPU, compared to a baseline model. We do this by analysing a baseline model to find what parts can be compressed without excessively losing accuracy. Our improved model has an inference time of 41 ms with a Mean Absolute Error (MEA) of 7.72 cm on an embedded device. Compared to the baseline this model saves 85.9% latency, at the cost of 4.8% MEA accuracy. Through finding which part can be compressed most effectively, we also gain insight in the relative importance of each component of the model. We also identify components that with further research could be improved to increase the accuracy of the model.

## FMCW毫米波雷达传感器三维检测与跟踪链的实验验证 [C]
